# Helix Biosciences — Quarterly Open-Lab & Vendor Symposium

## Event Overview
On March 14, 2026, Helix Biosciences will host its Quarterly Open-Lab & Vendor Symposium at the Cambridge Innovation Hub. The goal is to align internal R&D teams with external partners on accelerating cell-line development pipelines. Dr. Elena Vasquez, VP of Process Development, will deliver the keynote at 09:30. We expect 140 attendees, including representatives from Thermo Fisher, Sartorius, and Bio-Rad. Registration closes on March 7, 2026.

## Agenda and Schedule
The program runs from 08:00 to 16:30. Morning sessions focus on upstream bioprocessing, while the afternoon shifts to analytical validation and regulatory strategy.

- 08:00 — Check-in and continental breakfast
- 09:30 — Keynote: Scaling Mammalian Culture to 2,000 L
- 11:00 — Panel: Single-Use Technologies and Contamination Risk
- 13:00 — Networking lunch sponsored by MilliporeSigma
- 14:15 — Workshop: qPCR Assay Qualification Under ICH Q2(R1)
- 16:00 — Closing remarks and facility tour

## Venue and Logistics
The event takes place in Building 4, Conference Suites A and B. Parking passes are available for Lot C; please request them by March 10. AV support will be managed by the in-house IT team led by Marcus Chen. We will provide wireless lapel mics, two 85-inch displays, and live captioning. Temperature-controlled storage for demonstration reagents is available in Room 402.

## Safety and Compliance
All guests must complete the biosafety orientation before entering BSL-2 zones. Lab coats, safety glasses, and nitrile gloves are mandatory in demonstration areas. Emergency contacts are posted at every exit, and the nearest eyewash station is 15 meters from Suite A. Visitors should report any spill immediately to the Safety Officer on duty, James Okonkwo.

## Follow-Up Actions
Within 48 hours of the event, attendees will receive a summary deck and vendor contact sheets. The Process Development group will schedule follow-up meetings with Thermo Fisher and Sartorius by April 1, 2026. Feedback surveys must be submitted by March 21 to qualify for the post-event webinar series.
