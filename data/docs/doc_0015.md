# Onboarding Guide: Launching with NordCommerce

Welcome to NordCommerce. This guide walks new merchants through account activation, catalog setup, and first-sale readiness within 14 days.

## Account Verification

Before listing products, complete identity verification. Submit your business registration and a government-issued ID for the primary account holder. Our compliance team, led by reviewer Sarah Chen, typically approves documents within 48 hours. You will receive a confirmation email at the address tied to your NordCommerce ID.

## Store Configuration

Set your storefront preferences in the Dashboard. Configure tax rules for your operating region, enable Stripe and PayPal gateways, and choose your default currency. We recommend reviewing the brand style guide released on March 12, 2024, to ensure banners align with platform visual standards.

## Product Catalog Setup

Add items via CSV import or manual entry. Each listing requires at least one high-resolution image (minimum 1200x1200 px), a SKU, and accurate inventory counts. Key catalog rules include:

- Titles must stay under 80 characters
- Descriptions should include material, dimensions, and care instructions
- Set a reorder threshold of 10 units for auto-alerts
- Use up to 5 tags per product for search visibility

## Shipping and Fulfillment

Integrate with our logistics partners, including ShipFast and DHL Express, to generate real-time rates. Set handling times to 2 business days or less to maintain your Fast Shipper badge. Print labels directly from the Orders panel and schedule daily pickup windows between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM local time.

## Launch Checklist and Support

Before going live, confirm that your return policy is published, your support email is monitored, and your first 25 products are active. Reach the merchant success team at support@nordcommerce.com or through live chat, available Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST. Your dedicated onboarding manager, Marcus Webb, will schedule your 30-day performance review to review conversion metrics and growth targets.

Once these steps are complete, toggle your store to Public mode and begin accepting orders.
