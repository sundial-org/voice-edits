# MetroLine Service Update: Faster Connections Starting October 14

MetroLine is committed to making every trip smoother, faster, and more reliable for the communities we serve. After months of planning with city partners and rider feedback from over 12,000 surveys, we are excited to share the next phase of our Network Redesign initiative. This update reflects real input from passengers across all neighborhoods.

## What's Changing on Route 42

Beginning Monday, October 14, 2024, Route 42 will shift from 20-minute to 15-minute headways during weekday peak hours. We are also extending evening service until 11:30 p.m. on Fridays and Saturdays to better support downtown workers and late-night riders. Buses will now serve the new Eastside Transit Hub, providing direct transfers to the Green Line and the regional bike-share network.

These adjustments come after extensive traffic pattern analysis led by Transit Planning Director Maria Chen and her team. They identified bottlenecks near Riverside Drive and worked with the Department of Public Works to implement dedicated bus lanes that should reduce average travel time by six minutes per trip.

## Key Rider Benefits

Our goal is not just more buses, but a better experience from curb to destination. Riders on the updated network can expect:

- Shorter wait times with 15-minute frequency on Route 42 during morning and evening peaks
- Direct access to the Eastside Transit Hub without requiring a second fare
- Extended Friday and Saturday service until 11:30 p.m., aligning with local business hours
- Real-time arrival displays installed at twelve high-ridership stops by November 1

We also heard clearly that accessibility matters. Every vehicle assigned to Route 42 now features low-floor boarding and priority seating indicators that exceed ADA guidelines.

## Implementation Timeline

The rollout will happen in stages to minimize disruption. On October 14, new schedules and the Eastside Hub connection go live. The dedicated bus lanes near Riverside Drive will be painted by October 28, with enforcement beginning November 4. Real-time signage installation will wrap up by November 1.

If you have questions, our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m., and you can reach them at 555-METRO or through the MetroLine app. We will host an open house at the Eastside Transit Hub on October 10 from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. for anyone who wants to see the new layout firsthand.

## Looking Ahead

This redesign is just one step. By early 2025, we plan to evaluate similar upgrades for Routes 17 and 89 based on the data we collect this fall. Thank you for riding with us, and thank you for helping shape a transit system that works for everyone.
