# Welcome to Tidal Drift Studio — Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Tidal Drift Studio. We are a twelve-person indie team spread across Portland, Berlin, and Tokyo, founded in March 2019 by designers Lena Vasquez and Marcus Okonkwo. This guide will orient you before your first sprint on Project Saltline.

## Your First Week

Your onboarding begins on a Monday to align with our biweekly cycle. By Friday, you should have commit access to the Saltline repository, a calibrated Wacom Cintiq Pro 24, and a 1:1 with your discipline lead. We work in focused blocks: core collaboration runs 10:00 to 15:00 Pacific, with async updates expected by 18:00 UTC.

- Introduce yourself in #general with your timezone and one non-work hobby
- Clone the Saltline build and verify it runs on the reference rig (RTX 4080, 64 GB RAM)
- Read the Living Design Doc version 4.2 and leave at least three comments
- Schedule your security briefing with IT by Wednesday at the latest

## Tools and Workflow

We ship in Unreal Engine 5.3 with Perforce Helix Core for source control. Art lives in Blender 4.1 and Substance 3D Painter. Communication happens in Discord; project tracking lives in Linear. Do not commit binary files over 50 MB without approval from Lena Vasquez.

## Culture and Communication

We value direct feedback over hierarchy. Every Thursday at 14:00 Pacific we hold a thirty-minute retrospective open to all disciplines. If you spot a blocker, flag it in #saltline-alerts within fifteen minutes rather than waiting for the next standup. Remote members in Berlin and Tokyo rotate attendance so no single timezone carries the late-night burden.

## Getting Help

Questions about engine pipeline go to Marcus Okonkwo. Narrative or quest design routes to Senior Writer Aiko Tanaka. For HR, equipment, or payroll issues, contact Operations Lead Diego Ramos at diego@tidaldrift.studio. We expect a response within one business day; urgent build failures should ping the on-call engineer immediately.

We are glad you are here. Your first milestone review is in thirty days. Let's build something strange and beautiful together.
