# Meridian Freight Internal FAQ — Operations & Dispatch

## Onboarding and System Access
New team members must complete Meridian University Module 4 before receiving dispatcher credentials. All access requests go through the IT portal managed by Javier Torres. If your badge does not scan at Gate B after 6:00 p.m., call Security at ext. 4100. Remember that the warehouse management system updates every night at 02:00 EST, so avoid scheduling bulk imports between 01:30 and 03:15.

## Shift Coverage and Escalation
When a driver reports a mechanical failure on Route 77, open a Tier-2 ticket immediately. Do not attempt to reroute loads without notifying the Night Supervisor, Dana Reeves. Our peak season begins November 14, 2024, and runs through January 3, 2025; during that window, overtime approvals require sign-off from both the floor manager and HR.

- Confirm the Bill of Lading number in ScanTrack before sealing any trailer.
- Photograph all visible damage using the Meridian app, not personal devices.
- Notify the consignee within 45 minutes if delivery will miss the 18:00 window.
- Escalate temperature-controlled loads immediately if the reefer unit reads above 38 °F.

## Safety, Compliance, and Audit Prep
Every quarter, the Safety Committee led by Marcus Chen reviews incident logs and updates the checklist posted in the break room. Forklift recertification is mandatory every 24 months; the next group session is scheduled for March 22, 2025, in Bay 7. Keep your DOT medical card current—expired cards will result in immediate suspension from active routes until HR clears the renewal.

## Customer Exceptions and Chargebacks
If a recipient refuses a partial shipment, document the refusal reason in the client notes and flag the account for review. Chargeback requests must include the original delivery timestamp, the photo of the signed receipt, and a brief explanation from the dispatcher on duty. The Finance team processes adjustments every Tuesday and Thursday; submissions received after 11:00 a.m. on Thursday roll to the following week. For disputes involving accounts over $50,000, loop in Regional Director Sarah Kim before responding.
