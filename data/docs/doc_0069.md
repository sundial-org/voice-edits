# Product Requirements Document: Meridian Commerce Platform v2.0

## Overview
Meridian Commerce is rebuilding its checkout and inventory backbone to support 2.4 million monthly active users across North America. The initiative, code-named Project Aurora, targets a full production release on March 15, 2025.

## Goals and Success Metrics
We need to reduce cart abandonment from 34 percent to under 22 percent and increase average order value by 12 percent. Engineering lead Sarah Chen will oversee delivery, while Product Manager David Park owns the roadmap.

## Functional Requirements
The platform must deliver:
- Real-time inventory sync across 14 distribution centers
- One-click reorder using saved payment tokens
- Dynamic tax calculation for all 50 US states and 10 provinces
- Guest checkout with optional account creation post-purchase

## Technical Specifications
Services will run on Kubernetes 1.29 with a target p99 latency below 180 milliseconds. The database layer migrates from PostgreSQL 13 to PostgreSQL 16 on February 28, 2025, with zero-downtime replication.

## Launch Plan
Beta access begins January 10, 2025 for 5,000 invite-only merchants. General availability follows the March 15 milestone after security audits by CyberShield Labs conclude.

## Risks and Dependencies
Dependency on Stripe Connect for marketplace payouts introduces compliance risk in Canada. We will maintain a fallback gateway to ensure 99.99 percent uptime during peak traffic on Black Friday 2025.
