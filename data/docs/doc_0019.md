# IT Onboarding Guide — Riverside General Hospital

Welcome to the Information Technology division at Riverside General Hospital. Our team supports clinical, administrative, and research systems across the main campus and two satellite clinics. This guide outlines the protocols, tools, and expectations for your first 30 days.

## Security and Compliance

Every new hire must complete HIPAA and cybersecurity training before receiving production credentials. Dr. Elena Vasquez, Chief Information Security Officer, requires all staff to finish the LearnShield modules by March 14, 2024. You will receive an encrypted laptop, a smart badge for the data center, and a YubiKey for multi-factor authentication. Do not share badges or store patient data on personal devices. Violations are reported immediately to the compliance office.

## Core Systems and Access

Your primary platforms include Epic Hyperspace, Cerner Radiology, and the internal ServiceNow portal. Marcus Chen, Senior Systems Architect, will schedule your provisioning session within 72 hours of your start date. You will be granted read-only access initially, with elevated privileges after shadowing a senior engineer for two full shifts. Remember to keep your password manager updated and rotate credentials every 90 days.

## On-Call Procedures and Escalation

The IT group maintains 24/7 coverage using a tiered rotation. Primary on-call runs from 4:00 PM to 8:00 AM on weekdays and covers the full weekend. If a critical system fails—defined as any outage affecting patient monitoring or pharmacy dispensing—escalate within 15 minutes using the PagerDuty chain. Non-urgent tickets should be documented with patient-impact notes and assigned by 9:00 AM each business day.

## First-Week Checklist

To stay on track during onboarding, complete the following tasks:

- Attend the orientation session in Building C, Room 302
- Submit your background verification to HR by day three
- Configure your VPN profile and test remote access
- Shadow the overnight network operations team
- Review the 2024 disaster recovery playbook with your mentor
- Confirm your badge activates the server room elevator bank

## Getting Help

Questions are expected. Reach out to your assigned mentor, the #it-help Slack channel, or the service desk at extension 4400. The team holds a brief stand-up every morning at 8:30 AM in the third-floor lounge. We value precision, calm under pressure, and respect for clinical staff who depend on our infrastructure. Welcome to the team.
