# Resonance Collective — Studio Log & Release Changelog

## Q3 Harvest: Analog Summing Chain Upgrade
On August 14 we replaced the aging Neve 8078 patchbay with a custom 32-channel discrete summing matrix built by Elias Vance. The noise floor dropped 4 dB across the master bus, and low-mid buildup in the 240 Hz pocket tightened noticeably. We rerouted all six drum subgroups through the new path and documented the change in file `SUM-2024-08.rev`. Members can now book the chain in two-hour blocks.

## Collective Roster Update
We welcomed three new producers this quarter:
- Mira Okonkwo, specializing in granular field recordings
- Jonas Reed, modular synthesis and live coding
- Priya Desai, mix engineering and spatial audio design

Their first collaborative session is slated for September 22 at the Warehouse North location.

## Firmware & Tooling Changes
- Updated the UAD Apollo X16 drivers to v11.3.2 on Studio B and Studio C
- Retired the legacy 2019 sample library; replaced with 847 GB of new acoustic captures from Copenhagen and Tokyo
- Calibrated all monitoring chains to 83 dB SPL C-weighted slow

## Upcoming Release Pipeline
The collective will drop two records before year-end. "Glass Tides" by Okonkwo and Reed lands October 9. Desai's immersive mix of "Substrate Drift" follows on November 17. Both will be available in binaural, 5.1, and stereo formats.
