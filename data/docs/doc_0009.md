# Vanguard Commerce: Q4 Peak-Readiness Event Plan

## Overview and Objectives
The Vanguard Commerce platform will execute its annual Q4 Peak-Readiness event from October 14 through October 18, 2024. The primary objective is to validate system stability under simulated Black Friday loads of up to 850,000 requests per minute while onboarding three strategic merchants, including Northwind Electronics. Engineering, SRE, and Merchant Success will coordinate through a centralized war room in the Seattle headquarters.

## Timeline and Milestones
Preparation begins September 9 with architecture reviews and load-test scripting. By September 30, all failover clusters must replicate data across US-East and EU-West regions. The live simulation window opens at 09:00 UTC on October 14 and closes at 17:00 UTC on October 18. A go/no-go review is scheduled for October 11 at 14:00 UTC with CTO Elena Vasquez and VP of Operations Marcus Lee.

## Technical Preparation
Infrastructure teams will complete the following checklist before the simulation begins:
- Scale Kubernetes pods to 240 nodes across production clusters
- Enable circuit-breaker thresholds at 92% CPU and 78% memory utilization
- Validate payment-gateway redundancy with Stripe and Adyen endpoints
- Run chaos-engineering drills targeting the catalog caching layer

## Risk Mitigation and Monitoring
Real-time dashboards will track latency, error rates, and checkout funnel abandonment. If p99 latency exceeds 450 milliseconds for more than three consecutive minutes, the SRE team will initiate graceful traffic shedding to the secondary region. Incident commander rotation covers 24-hour shifts led by senior engineers Priya Nair, James Okonkwo, and Sarah Kim.

## Post-Event Review and Reporting
Findings will be published in an internal retrospective by November 5, 2024. Merchants will receive a readiness certificate and personalized scaling recommendations by November 12. All playbooks updated during the event will be archived in the Vanguard Knowledge Base for reuse during the Cyber Monday window on December 2.
