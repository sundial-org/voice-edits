# Vertex BioLabs — Changelog & Protocol Revisions

## Q1 Bioreactor Calibration (Dr. Elena Vasquez, 14 Feb 2024)
Dr. Vasquez led recalibration of the 50 L single-use bioreactor fleet after dissolved-oxygen probes showed a 12 % drift across Batch A-117. New calibration curves were validated against NIST-traceable standards, reducing sensor variance to under 1.5 %. All technicians must now run the two-point verification before every inoculation.

## Updated Cell Culture SOP v4.2
The revised SOP, effective 01 March 2024, replaces the 2019 manual. Key updates include:
- Mandatory mycoplasma screening every 72 hours for all CHO-K1 lines
- Reduced FBS concentration from 10 % to 7.5 % in maintenance media
- Introduction of barcode tracking for flasks exceeding passage 18
- Updated cryopreservation protocol using 10 % DMSO with controlled-rate freezing at -1 °C/min
- Requirement for dual-witness sign-off on all gene-editing steps

## Facility & Equipment
On 22 March 2024, Lab 3B received the new Thermo Fisher Heracell VIOS 250i incubator, bringing total CO2-controlled capacity to 240 L. The old 2009 model was decommissioned and sent for metal recovery.

## Safety & Compliance
No reportable incidents in Q1. The biosafety committee approved amendments to BSL-2 waste-stream labeling, requiring orange tags for all viral-vector byproducts generated after 15 April 2024.

## Upcoming Milestones
Dr. James Okonkwo will present preliminary yield data from the 500 L pilot run at the ISPE conference on 18 May 2024. Target titer for mAb-442 remains 3.2 g/L.
