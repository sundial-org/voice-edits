# Research Notes — Northbridge Marketplace Q3 Optimization

## Executive Summary
Internal review of checkout funnel drop-offs on Northbridge Marketplace conducted between August 12 and September 4, 2024. Lead analyst Marisol Vega flagged a 14.3% abandonment spike at the shipping calculator stage. Competitor audit included data from Cartwheel and Meridian Retail.

## User Behavior Metrics
Traffic remained stable at 42,000 weekly sessions, but conversion dipped to 2.1%. Heatmap recordings from FullStory revealed repeated interaction failures on the promo-code field.

Key observations from session replays:
- 68% of mobile users zoomed before tapping the apply button
- Average time-to-checkout exceeded 4 minutes and 18 seconds
- 31% of users who reached payment never completed the order

## Technical Debt & Performance
Server response latency averaged 840 ms during peak hours on August 29, correlating with a deployment of the new recommendation engine v2.1. Engineering lead Derek Ho recommended rolling back the algorithm until caching is optimized.

## Competitive Benchmarking
Meridian Retail launched one-click checkout on July 22, reducing their mobile abandonment to 6.8%. Cartwheel added Buy Now, Pay Later integration via Klarna in mid-August, which lifted average order value by $18.

## Next Steps
- Prioritize mobile resize and touch-target updates by October 3
- Conduct A/B test on collapsed shipping options (target: 50/50 split)
- Schedule architecture review with Derek Ho for September 18
