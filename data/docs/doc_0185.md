# Welcome to Resonance Collective

Welcome to Resonance Collective, a music-production cooperative founded in March 2019 by producers Maya Chen, Leo Park, and engineer Nico Alvarez. Our studio, located in the Eastside Arts District, operates on a shared-resources model designed to keep overhead low and creativity high.

## Getting Started

Before you book your first session, complete the onboarding checklist in the shared Notion workspace. You will need to submit a signed liability waiver, confirm your keycard access with Nico by 5:00 PM on Friday, and upload a 30-second demo reel to the member portal. Once approved, you can reserve Studio A or Studio B through the calendar system up to 14 days in advance.

- Submit your liability waiver and profile photo
- Confirm keycard pickup with Nico by Friday at 5:00 PM
- Upload a 30-second demo reel to the portal
- Reserve Studio A or B via the shared calendar

New members receive 8 hours of complimentary studio time during their first month. After that, pricing follows a tiered structure: $18 per hour for off-peak blocks (10:00 AM to 4:00 PM) and $32 per hour for prime-time slots (6:00 PM to midnight).

## Studio Protocols

Respect for shared gear is non-negotiable. Always power down the Neve 1073 preamp after use, replace any used cables in the labeled bins, and log every session in the physical binder at the console. If you encounter technical issues—whether it is a ground loop on the Moog Sub 37 or a corrupted session on the main iMac—document it immediately in the #maintenance Slack channel.

## Collaboration & Credits

We encourage cross-pollination. If you work with another member's stems or feature a guest vocalist, agree on split percentages before export. All releases that use collective resources must include the credit line "Produced at Resonance Collective" and list contributing engineers in the metadata.

## Community

We are excited to hear what you build here. Welcome aboard.
