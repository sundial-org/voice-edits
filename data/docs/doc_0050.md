# Iron Lark Games — Internal FAQ

## Studio Basics
Iron Lark Games is a remote-first indie studio founded in 2019 by Mira Chen and Darius Okonkwo. We are currently twelve people spread across four time zones. Our flagship title, *Saltbound*, launched on Steam in March 2023 and has sold over 180,000 copies. We operate without a traditional publisher, funding development through a mix of revenue share and the 2022 Indie Fund grant.

## Release Timeline & Milestones
The *Saltbound* 1.4 "Tidal Lock" update is targeted for October 14, 2024. The console port contract with Portside Interactive expires on December 1, 2024, so QA must be finalized by November 8. If we miss the November milestone, we risk delaying the Switch version into Q1 2025.

## Communication Protocols
We use Discord for real-time chat, Notion for documentation, and GitHub for everything code-related. Stand-up is at 10:00 AM ET every Tuesday and Thursday. Deep-work blocks are protected on Mondays and Wednesdays—no meetings allowed unless it is a production emergency defined in the handbook.

## Asset Pipeline & Tools
All art must pass through the three-stage review:
- Concept approval by Mira Chen
- Technical check in Blender 4.1 or Aseprite 1.3
- Final export to the shared Perforce depot by Friday 6:00 PM ET
Audio is handled in Reaper; sound design requests should be filed in the "Audio Backlog" Notion board with a 48-hour lead time.

## Remote Work & Expenses
We reimburse up to $1,200 per year for home office equipment. Team retreats happen twice annually; the next one is scheduled for February 2025 in Lisbon. If you need to travel for a conference like GDC or Gamescom, submit the request to Darius at least 60 days in advance.

## HR & Confidentiality
Payroll runs on the 15th and the last business day of each month. Health benefits are administered through Zenith and cover dental starting at $0 copay for preventive care. Remember that all player telemetry, unreleased build footage, and financial forecasts are strictly confidential per your NDA signed on your start date.
