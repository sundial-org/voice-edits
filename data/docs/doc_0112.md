# Orion Reach Astronomy Club — Weekly Status Update: October 14

## Observing Report
The past week delivered exceptional transparency across three consecutive nights. On October 11, club members Elena Vasquez and Marcus Chen logged four hours at the North Ridge dark site, tracking the Andromeda Galaxy and the Double Cluster in Perseus through 8-inch Dobsonians. Seeing averaged 1.2 arcseconds, allowing crisp resolution of M31's dust lanes. A total of twelve observers attended, including two first-time guests from Ridgeview High.

## Upcoming Events

Please confirm your availability with Dr. Priya Malhotra by October 20 so we can finalize the equipment roster.

## Equipment and Maintenance
The club's new 102mm apochromatic refractor arrived on October 9 and has been collimated. We also performed routine maintenance on the 14-inch Schmidt-Cassegrain:
- Replaced the right-ascension drive belt
- Cleaned the corrector plate with distilled solution
- Updated the hand controller firmware to version 4.2
- Rebalanced the mount for winter payloads

## Education Corner
This week's session, led by Dr. Malhotra, covered variable star photometry using data from the AAVSO database. Seven members submitted preliminary light curves for Delta Cephei. Next Tuesday's workshop focuses on planetary imaging with a live demonstration using a ZWO ASI224MC camera.

## Member Spotlight
Congratulations to junior member Aisha Okonkwo, who submitted her first Messier Marathon checklist on October 12, completing 87 objects in a single session. Her observation notes on M57 and M27 have been added to our shared digital logbook.
