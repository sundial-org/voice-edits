# Technical Design Document: Echoes of Hollowreach

## Overview
Echoes of Hollowreach is a 2.5D atmospheric exploration game developed by Thistleweed Interactive. The target release window is Q3 2026 on PC via Steam and itch.io. We are building in Godot 4.3 with a custom shader pipeline targeting 60 FPS on hardware equivalent to an NVIDIA GTX 1660.

## Core Mechanics
The player controls Lyra, a cartographer who traverses collapsed biomes by inverting local gravity through relic activation. Key systems include:
- Procedural path generation seeded from player trajectory
- Sound-based puzzle triggers using real-time FFT analysis
- A persistent world clock that advances only during exploration
- Relic durability lasting exactly 14 minutes of active use before requiring a sanctuary recharge

## Art and Audio Pipeline
Visual direction draws from 19th-century survey illustrations combined with volumetric fog rendered in 1080p native resolution. Sound design is led by Marisol Vega, who will record over 400 foley samples in a decommissioned water tower outside Berlin.

## Milestones and Deliverables
The vertical slice is due March 15, 2025. Alpha builds ship to our closed testing pool of 120 backers by August 1, 2025. Beta begins February 2026, with a feature freeze on December 12, 2025 to allow three months of polish and localization into English, Japanese, and Brazilian Portuguese.

## Risk Assessment
Primary risks include shader compilation stutter on AMD integrated graphics and scope creep in the procedural generation system. We mitigate these with weekly profiling on a Ryzen 5 5600G test bench and a hard cap of 7 biome templates for the 1.0 release.
