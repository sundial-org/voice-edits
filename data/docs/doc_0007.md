# Internal FAQ — Aurora Mech 2025 Season

## Team Structure and Leads
Aurora Mech operates under a flat hierarchy led by Captain Elena Vasquez and Technical Director Marcus Chen. We split work into Mechanical, Software, and Operations pods. All major design reviews happen on Thursdays at 16:00 in Lab B.

## Build Schedule and Deadlines
Our regional kickoff is March 14. The chassis must be welded by April 2, and full system integration is locked for April 22. We run stress tests every Tuesday and Friday until ship day on May 5.

## Software Stack and Tools
The codebase lives in GitHub under `aurora-mech/firmware-2025`. We use ROS 2 Humble, Python 3.11, and a custom CAN bridge written by Dr. Priya Nair. Every commit requires a peer review from at least one Software pod member.

- Clone the repo with `git clone` after joining the team drive
- Run `colcon build` in the `ws/` directory before flashing
- Tag releases using `vYYYY.MM.DD` (e.g., v2025.04.22)
- Document sensor calibrations in `docs/calibration.md`

## Safety and Lab Access
No solo work after 22:00. Safety goggles and closed-toe shoes are mandatory inside the build zone. The first-aid kit is mounted near the lathe, and incident reports must be filed within 24 hours.

## Competition Logistics
We travel to the Detroit Regional on May 9–11. The truck leaves campus at 06:00 on May 8. Each traveler needs a signed waiver and a confirmed meal plan by April 28. Contact Logistics Lead Jordan Patel for seat assignments.
