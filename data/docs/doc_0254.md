# Weekly Status Update — Orbit DevTools

## Product & Engineering
This week we shipped v2.4.0 of the Orbit CLI. Marcus Chen rewrote the dependency graph resolver, cutting cold-start times from 4.2s to 1.1s for repos over 500 packages. We also patched a race condition in the live-reload daemon that was affecting users on macOS Sonoma.

- Resolved 14 open tickets in the build-pipeline backlog
- Added support for pnpm 9.1 and Yarn 4.2 lockfiles
- Deployed canary builds to 23 internal dogfood users
- Updated CI images to Node 21.6 LTS

## Growth & Metrics
Sign-ups hit 1,847 for the week ending March 15, up 12% from the prior week. Our paid conversion rate ticked up to 4.8%. The biggest driver was the "Zero-config monorepo" landing page launched on March 12.

We also noticed churn dropping to 2.1% after releasing the new onboarding checklist.

## Team & Operations
Sarah Kim joined as Head of Developer Relations. She previously led community at Vercel and will own docs, Discord, and our upcoming March 28 webinar on "Fast CI for TypeScript."

The team is now 17 people. We finalized office access in the Mission District and moved our weekly sync to Tuesdays at 10:00 AM PT.

## Upcoming Milestones
Next week we begin the closed beta for Orbit Cloud, targeting 50 design partners. Engineering will freeze features by March 22 to focus on load testing. We'll share the Q1 roadmap publicly on March 25.
