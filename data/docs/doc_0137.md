# Metro Transit Authority — Weekly Operations Update

## Service Performance Overview
For the week ending Friday, October 13, 2023, system-wide on-time performance reached 91.4 percent, up 2.1 points from the prior seven-day period. Bus routes averaged 12,340 daily boardings across the core network, while the Red Line rail corridor carried 38,200 passengers on Thursday alone. These figures reflect stable ridership growth heading into the fall schedule.

## Infrastructure & Maintenance
Crews completed overnight track replacement along the Eastbound Blue Line between Riverside Station and Commerce Hub, restoring full-speed operations by 05:30 on Wednesday, October 11. Station maintenance at Central Square focused on escalator rehabilitation and LED lighting upgrades in the mezzanine concourse. All work stayed within the $240,000 capital allocation approved at the September board meeting.

## Rider Experience & Communications
Customer feedback through the MetroConnect portal increased to 312 submissions this week, with 78 percent related to real-time arrival accuracy. We deployed updated digital signage at 14 high-ridership stops, including Union Terminal and Westfield Mall, displaying live train positions and service alerts in both English and Spanish.

## Upcoming Service Adjustments
Effective Monday, October 16, the Green Line will shift to a 10-minute headway during midday hours, replacing the previous 12-minute interval. Weekend bus detours on Route 42 will remain in place through November 5 due to sewer repairs on Maple Avenue. Riders should consult the trip planner for updated departure times.

## Key Metrics This Week
- Red Line reliability: 96.7 percent
- Average bus delay: 3.2 minutes
- Completed maintenance orders: 847
- Customer satisfaction score: 4.1 out of 5.0
