# E-Commerce Platform Research Notes — Q3 Optimization

## Context & Objectives
This document consolidates findings from the Q3 performance audit conducted between July 14 and August 29, 2024. Our goal is to understand friction points in the checkout funnel for the Meridian storefront, particularly on mobile devices where 64% of sessions originate.

## Key Metrics & Benchmarks
Data pulled from Analytics Cloud on September 3, 2024 reveals a conversion rate of 2.3%, down 0.4 points from Q2. Average order value held steady at $87. Meanwhile, cart abandonment spiked to 71.2% on weekends, suggesting payment gateway latency.

- Mobile load time exceeded 3.2 seconds on 3G networks
- Payment failures peaked at 8.7% during the August 19 flash sale
- Customer support tickets referencing checkout errors rose by 142 in the final two weeks

## User Feedback Synthesis
Interviews with 18 participants conducted by Dr. Elena Vasquez highlighted confusion around the discount code field. Several users expected automatic application rather than manual entry, a pattern also noted in 34 support transcripts.

## Technical Debt & Infrastructure
The legacy payments module relies on Stripe API v2018-11-22, which will reach end-of-life on January 15, 2025. Upgrading to the 2024-06-20 version is non-negotiable to maintain PCI compliance.

## Next Steps
Engineering will prioritize gateway redundancy and A/B test a streamlined checkout by October 10, 2024. Marketing will coordinate with the Meridian team to align discount messaging across channels before the holiday rollout.
