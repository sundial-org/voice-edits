# Product Requirements Document: TerraLink Carbon Ledger 2.0

## Overview
TerraLink is building an enterprise-grade carbon accounting platform for supply-chain visibility. This document defines the requirements for the Carbon Ledger 2.0 release, targeting general availability by March 15, 2025.

## Objectives
The release must reduce audit preparation time by 40% for clients like Meridian Logistics and Apex Materials. We will integrate real-time Scope 3 emission factors from the Global Carbon Registry v4.2 and support automated disclosure formatting aligned with the ISSB S2 standard.

## Key Features
- Emission factor auto-update via API with 99.95% uptime SLA
- Supplier-tier mapping up to Tier 4 with geospatial tagging
- Audit trail export in XBRL, PDF, and CSV formats
- Role-based access controls for sustainability officers and external auditors

## Technical Requirements
The backend will run on Kubernetes 1.29 across three regions: Frankfurt, Singapore, and US-East-1. Database writes must complete within 200ms at the 95th percentile. All PII must be encrypted using AES-256-GCM, and logs retained for 7 years per EU CSRD mandates.

## Milestones
- Alpha: November 4, 2024
- Beta with Meridian Logistics: January 22, 2025
- GA: March 15, 2025

## Risks and Mitigations
Regulatory divergence between the EU CSRD and SEC climate disclosure rules could split our schema design. We will maintain a modular taxonomy layer and assign Dr. Elena Vasquez as regulatory lead to review changes weekly.
