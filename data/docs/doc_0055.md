# Orion Astronomy Club — Weekly Status Update (Week of Oct 14)

## Observing Session Recap
Last Saturday, October 12, twelve members gathered at Maple Ridge Dark Sky Preserve for our monthly public outreach night. Despite early cloud cover rolling in around 21:30, we managed three solid hours of viewing through a 10-inch Dobsonian and Dr. Elena Vasquez’s 80 mm refractor. The Andromeda Galaxy and the Double Cluster in Perseus drew the largest crowds. We logged 47 visitors and distributed 30 star charts printed by our outreach committee. Special thanks to Marcus Chen for coordinating parking and safety lighting.

## Equipment and Maintenance Notes
The club’s 8-inch Schmidt-Cassegrain needs a collimation check before the Hilltop trip. James Okonkwo volunteered to handle alignment on Wednesday, October 23. We also received two donated eyepieces—a 9 mm Plössl and a 15 mm wide-field—thanks to retired member Robert Hale. These will be added to the lending library after inspection.
