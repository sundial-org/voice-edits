# Product Requirements Document: Black Oak Coffee Roastery — Q3 Small-Batch Release

## Overview
Black Oak Coffee Roastery will launch the "Summit Series" in Q3 2025, featuring three origin-specific micro-lots roasted in our Portland facility. Led by Head Roaster Elena Vargas, the initiative targets specialty cafes in the Pacific Northwest with traceable, high-scoring beans.

## Product Specifications
Each Summit Series bag must meet strict parameters before release:
- Roast profile: medium-light, Agtron 62–68
- Bag size: 12 oz (340 g) with one-way degassing valve
- Packaging: compostable kraft with lot number printed by July 15, 2025
- Shelf stability: 90 days post-roast with nitrogen-flushed sealing

## Sourcing & Quality Targets
We will secure two direct-trade contracts and one cooperative lot:
- Finca La Esperanza, Guatemala (1,200 lbs, 87.5 cupping score)
- Worka Cooperative, Ethiopia (800 lbs, 89.0 cupping score)
- Mount Elgon Estate, Uganda (600 lbs, 85.5 cupping score)

All green coffee must arrive at our warehouse by June 20, 2025, and pass moisture testing below 11.5 percent.

## Release Timeline & Milestones
Production runs from July 1 through August 15, 2025. Key dates:
- June 30: green inventory confirmed
- July 22: first roast batches completed
- August 1: wholesale pricing finalized at $14.50 per bag
- August 20: public launch via our web store and three partner cafes in Seattle

## Go-to-Market Requirements
Marketing assets must include origin cards, brew guides, and QR-linked traceability pages. Elena Vargas will host two cupping events on August 5 and August 12 to train baristas on extraction targets: 1:16.5 ratio, 203°F water temperature, 28-second bloom.

## Success Metrics
We will measure performance against Q3 revenue targets of $48,000 and a 92 percent customer retention rate for wholesale accounts. Any batch falling below an 85-point quality threshold will be diverted to our cold-brew blend line rather than released under the Summit label.
