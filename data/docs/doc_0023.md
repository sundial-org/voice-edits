# SolVantage Installers — Q3 2024 Changelog

## Overview
The third quarter marked our largest residential rollout to date. Between July 1 and September 30, 2024, our crews completed 312 rooftop arrays across four counties, pushing cumulative installed capacity past 8.4 MW. Supply chain delays that plagued the spring eased in mid-August, allowing Project Lead Maria Chen to lock in a fixed-price module contract through Q1 2025. We also welcomed two new NABCEP-certified electricians, bringing field staff to 34 technicians.

## Q3 Deployments
- 142 kW commercial canopy at Riverside Logistics Park, commissioned August 12
- 89 residential systems in the Maple Ridge subdivision, average 9.8 kW per home
- 23 off-grid battery backups paired with HelioGrid 5000 inverters for rural clients
- 41 ground-mount arrays for agricultural cooperatives near Dalton County

The Riverside project required custom ballasted racking due to membrane roofing; engineering finalized load calculations by July 22, and the interconnection agreement with the local co-op was signed on September 5.

## Safety & Compliance Updates
No lost-time incidents were recorded in Q3. We revised fall-arrest protocols after an internal audit on August 29, and all crews completed refresher training by September 18. The new checklist integrates OSHA 1926.501 requirements directly into our digital work-order system. Inspector David Park noted zero critical deficiencies during the September 27 site review.

## Product & Vendor Changes
We transitioned primary module supply from SunPeak to Aurora PV 540W bifacial panels, reducing per-watt cost by $0.08 while improving low-light yield. Inventory now holds 2,400 panels in our regional warehouse. Inverter firmware was updated to version 4.2.1 on October 3, resolving the ground-fault reporting lag observed in earlier builds.

## Team & Operations
Scheduling moved to a centralized dispatch model on September 1, cutting average job-to-start time from 11 days to 6. Customer satisfaction scores averaged 4.8 out of 5 across 198 completed surveys. The annual company retreat is set for November 14, 2024, in Asheville.

## Upcoming Milestones
We are targeting 500 total installations by December 31, 2024, with a focus on commercial flat-roof retrofits. Permit pre-application for the Westfield Solar Farm (1.2 MW) is expected to submit before October 20.
