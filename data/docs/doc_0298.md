# Welcome to Meridian Health IT Onboarding

## First-Day Setup
Arrive at Building C by 8:00 a.m. on your start date—currently set for March 14, 2025. Check in with Security Officer Marcus Delgado to receive your badge and temporary parking pass. Once inside, report to the IT Operations Center on the fourth floor, Room 402. Your workstation will have a Dell Latitude laptop, dual monitors, and a pre-configured docking station. Log in with the temporary credentials printed on the sealed envelope at your desk; you must change your password within 2 hours.

## Access and Compliance
Healthcare IT requires strict adherence to HIPAA and internal security protocols. Before accessing the electronic health record (EHR) system, complete the mandatory Cybersecurity Awareness module, which takes approximately 90 minutes. You will also need to sign the Acceptable Use Agreement and the Data Breach Notification Acknowledgment form. Multi-factor authentication is enforced across all systems; enroll your mobile device using the Authenticator app by 12:00 p.m. on day one. Remember that all activity is audited—never share credentials, even with senior staff.

## Key Systems Overview
Familiarize yourself with the primary platforms within your first week:
- Epic Hyperspace for clinical workflow support and user provisioning
- ServiceNow for ticket management and change requests
- NetScaler VPN for remote access with a 4-hour session limit
- Veeam Backup Console for nightly data protection verification
- Splunk for security log monitoring and incident alerting
