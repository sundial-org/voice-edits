# Weekly Pulse — Collective Update (Oct 14–20)

## Studio Lock-Ins
The collective reserved Studio B for three consecutive nights, October 16 through October 18. Engineer Mara Delgado tracked bass and synth pads for the upcoming EP. By Friday evening, we had laid down twelve new stems across four songs, with the working title "Midnight Freight" emerging as the lead single.

## Mix Reviews
On Wednesday, October 16, the A&R circle met to review rough mixes. Key takeaways included tightening the low-end on "Glass Houses" and adding a field recording layer to "Neon Drift." The deadline for final vocal comps has been pushed to October 25.

## Upcoming Deadlines
- Mastering session booked for October 28 with Levin Audio
- Artwork proofs due back from designer Juno Park by October 22
- Press kit copy finalized and sent to distribution partner

## Community Notes
We welcomed two new members this week: producer Theo Vance and vocalist Amara Okonkwo. Their collaboration on the ambient interlude "Static Bloom" exceeded expectations, running just under four minutes and forty-three seconds.

## Next Week
The focus shifts to sequencing and crossfades. Expect a listening session on October 23. Keep communication in the shared thread so everyone stays aligned.
