# Meridian Freight Global Q3 Logistics Summit — Event Plan

## Overview and Objectives
Meridian Freight will host its Global Q3 Logistics Summit on September 14, 2024, at the Rotterdam Convention Center. The one-day event unites supply chain directors, port authorities, and technology vendors to address bottlenecks in North Sea routing and to launch the firm’s new carbon-tracking dashboard. With 340 registered attendees from 18 countries, the summit aims to finalize two strategic partnerships and secure pilot commitments for Q4.

## Venue, Schedule, and Staffing
Registration opens at 08:00, followed by the keynote at 09:30 delivered by Chief Operations Officer Elena Vasquez. Breakout sessions run in parallel tracks until 16:45, with a closing reception sponsored by NordPort Logistics. The venue provides 12 breakout rooms, 4 charging stations per hall, and dedicated customs documentation support for international guests arriving via Rotterdam The Hague Airport.

- 08:00 – Registration and welcome coffee in Hall B
- 09:30 – Keynote: “Resilient Corridors in 2025” by Elena Vasquez
- 11:00 – Track A (Automation) and Track B (Sustainability metrics)
- 13:15 – Networking lunch with regional port authority representatives
- 15:30 – Pilot program sign-up session for the carbon-tracking dashboard
- 17:15 – Closing reception at the waterfront terrace

## Technical and Safety Requirements
AV teams will deploy redundant fiber connections in all 12 breakout rooms, with a 30-second failover protocol tested by 07:45 on event day. Security screening follows ISO 28001 supply-chain security standards, and emergency evacuation routes are mapped to 3 designated assembly points. A medical station staffed by two paramedics will remain operational from 07:30 until 19:00.

## Budget and Vendor Coordination
The approved budget is €128,000, with 42% allocated to venue and catering, 28% to technology and broadcasting, and the remainder split between marketing, security, and contingency. Contracts with Rotterdam Convention Center and NordPort Logistics were executed by August 1, 2024, and all vendor payments are scheduled through the finance portal by August 22, 2024. A 10% contingency reserve covers last-minute freight disruptions or speaker travel adjustments.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 72 hours of closing, the events team will distribute session recordings, carbon-dashboard trial credentials, and a feedback survey to all 340 attendees. Pilot commitments collected on September 14 will be routed to the business development office by September 18, with contract drafts targeted for October 3, 2024. A debrief meeting with Elena Vasquez and the operations board is scheduled for September 20, 2024, to review attendance metrics and partnership outcomes.
