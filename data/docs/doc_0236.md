# Midnight Lantern Games — Veil of Tides

Midnight Lantern Games is a three-person indie studio founded in March 2019 by art director Elara Voss and systems designer Marcus Chen. Our debut title, Veil of Tides, is a narrative-driven exploration game set in a drowned archipelago where memory itself is currency.

We began prototyping in Unity 2021.3 LTS during the winter of 2022, moving to a custom Godot 4.2 pipeline after a six-month evaluation period. The game now targets a Q3 2025 release on Windows, macOS, and Steam Deck, with console ports planned for early 2026. Our budget sits at $180,000, funded by a mix of personal savings, the Indie Fund grant awarded in April 2023, and a modest Kickstarter that reached 340% of its $25,000 goal.

## Core Pillars

Every design decision in Veil of Tides serves one of three pillars:

- Emotional authenticity through unscripted dialogue trees with 14 recurring NPCs
- Environmental storytelling across 9 hand-painted biomes, each tied to a specific year in the archipelago's history
- Player agency without fail states—failure reshapes the world rather than ending the session

We reject traditional combat. Instead, tension builds through resource scarcity, tidal mechanics, and the constant risk of forgetting.

## Development Status

As of June 2025, the vertical slice is complete and we have entered full production. The first two biomes—Saltmere (1912) and Hollowwatch (1947)—are fully playable. Voice recording for the protagonist, recorded by actress Yuki Tanaka, wrapped in May. We are currently integrating our bespoke water simulation, which runs at a locked 60 frames per second on target hardware.

Our public demo launches on August 12, 2025. A closed beta for 500 backers follows on September 3.
