# Echo Chamber Collective Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Echo Chamber Collective. Since our founding in March 2018 by producer Maya Torres, we have operated as a shared workspace for electronic, ambient, and experimental artists. This document outlines everything you need to begin contributing safely and productively.

## Membership and Access

Your membership activates immediately upon signing the shared-resource agreement. We maintain a 48-hour booking window for Studio A and Studio B. All members receive one key fob programmed for 24/7 entry; report lost cards to Torres before 6:00 PM on weekdays.

- Verify your insurance documentation is current
- Complete the safety orientation in Studio C
- Reserve your first session through the internal portal
- Introduce yourself in the #general channel within 3 days

## Studio Protocols

Each room operates with specific technical standards. Studio A features the restored 1972 Neve console; Studio B runs fully in-the-box with calibrated monitors. Clean all patch bays after use. Label external drives with your name and the date—unclaimed storage is cleared on the first Monday of each month.

## Collaborative Workflow

We run on a 6-week release cycle. The collective expects every active member to contribute to at least one group compilation per quarter. Mixing feedback sessions occur every Thursday at 7:00 PM. Bring printed notes or a shared link; live playback is limited to 20 minutes per project to respect the schedule.

## Community Expectations

Respect is non-negotiable. Harassment, unauthorized sampling of another member’s work, or failure to maintain shared gear will result in immediate review. We celebrate risk-taking, but we demand accountability. If you witness a conflict, document it and notify the steering committee within 24 hours.

## First Steps

Book an orientation tour with Maya Torres by emailing onboarding@echochamber.studio. Your welcome packet includes a voucher for one free 3-hour block in Studio B. Use it within your first 30 days. After that, standard hourly rates apply, billed on the 15th and 30th.

We are glad you are here. Make noise with intention.
