# Internal FAQ — SentinelCore Cybersecurity (Q3 2024)

This document answers recurring operational questions for SentinelCore analysts, SOC leads, and engineering partners. Last reviewed by Dr. Elena Voss on 14 October 2024. If you find a contradiction, open a ticket in JIRA project SEC and tag the Threat Intelligence lead.

## Scope and Access

Who can request a full forensic image? Only Tier-2 analysts and above with manager approval. All requests must include the asset tag, business justification, and expected retention period. Images are stored in the cold vault for 90 days by default, then migrated to Glacier with AES-256 encryption. Never transfer raw images over public Wi-Fi or personal cloud drives.

## Incident Response Playbook

When do we invoke the SentinelCore Red Team? Invoke them when you observe confirmed lateral movement, ransomware artifacts, or unauthorized domain-admin changes. Do not wait for full triage. The Red Team must acknowledge within 15 minutes; if they miss the window, escalate to the CISO office.

Key timelines:
- Detection to containment: target under 4 hours
- Notification to legal: within 1 hour of confirmed breach
- Customer disclosure draft: completed within 72 hours per our Master Services Agreement

## Tooling and Telemetry

Our core stack is standardized to reduce alert fatigue and improve correlation speed.

- SentinelOne Singularity for endpoint detection and response
- Splunk Enterprise Security for SIEM correlation and dashboarding
- CrowdStrike Falcon for cloud workload protection
- Mandiant Advantage for threat-intelligence enrichment

If a tool is down, switch to the backup feed configured in the runbook. Never disable logging to "reduce noise." All telemetry is retained for 13 months to satisfy insurance and regulatory obligations.

## Compliance and Audits

We align with NIST 800-61 Rev. 2 and ISO 27035. Auditors review access logs quarterly; the next audit window opens on 2 November 2024. Prepare evidence bundles 48 hours in advance. Redact customer PII using the automated scrubbing pipeline—manual redaction is permitted only when the pipeline returns an error code.
