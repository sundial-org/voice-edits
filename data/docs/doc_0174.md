# Roastery R&D Notes — Q3 Origin Trials

## Ethiopian Test Batch Profile
We cupped the Yirgacheffe lot 47 on September 14 with head roaster Maria Chen. The sample showed jasmine at 18% moisture and a clean citric finish. Target development was 4:30 post-first-crack with an exit temperature of 212°C.

## Roast Curve Adjustments
The Loring S35 is behaving differently since the drum alignment on August 2. We need tighter control during the Maillard phase.

- Shorten preheat to 390°F instead of 410°F
- Extend drying phase by 45 seconds
- Drop at 218°C for brighter acidity
- Log gas adjustments every 30 seconds

## Sourcing Update: Colombia
Diego Ramirez confirmed the Huila micro-lot (15 bags, 70 kg each) arrives October 3. Cupping score averaged 86.5. Pricing is $4.80/lb FOB.

## Packaging & Shelf Life
Nitrogen flush tested at 2.1% residual O₂ on batch 0912. Sealed bags maintained cupping quality through week 6, but faded by day 52. Recommend 45-day rotation for retail.
