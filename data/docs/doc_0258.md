# Riverside General Hospital — IT Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Information Technology Services group at Riverside General Hospital. You are joining a team of 42 specialists who maintain clinical, administrative, and research infrastructure across three campuses. Our mission is zero-downtime support for patient-facing systems.

## Your First 48 Hours

Check in with Marcus Chen, IT Operations Manager, in Building C Room 302 by 9:00 a.m. on your start date. He will issue your proximity badge and laptop. Before you touch any network, complete the HIPAA Security Awareness module in the Learning Portal; this is mandatory and takes about 90 minutes.

Next, request accounts through the Identity Team. You will need:
- Active Directory login (domain: RGH-AD)
- Epic Hyperspace read-only access for training
- ServiceNow agent profile for ticket routing
- VPN token for remote clinical support

Your direct supervisor, Dr. Elena Vasquez, will schedule your orientation walkthrough for October 14, 2024.

## Access, Security, and Compliance

All staff must follow the 30-day credential rotation policy. Do not share badges or passwords. If you suspect a breach, call the Security Operations Center immediately at extension 5555.

Clinical workstations require dual-factor authentication via your badge plus biometric scan. Personal devices may connect only to the RGH-GUEST wireless network; never plug unknown USB drives into nursing station terminals.

Remember: patient data is protected under federal law and hospital policy. Unauthorized access—intentional or accidental—triggers an immediate review by the Compliance Office.

## Core Systems Overview

We support over 8,000 endpoints. Key platforms include:
- Epic electronic health records
- Philips IntelliVue patient monitoring network
- Pyxis medication dispensing interfaces
- Internal radiology PACS archive

Your first assignments will involve Tier-1 help desk coverage and shadow shifts in the data center. After 30 days, you may apply for specialized tracks in network engineering, clinical applications, or cybersecurity.

## Support Channels and Escalation

For urgent clinical system outages, page the on-call engineer through the paging system. Non-urgent requests go into ServiceNow with a 4-hour response target.

If you need guidance, reach out to the peer mentor assigned during your first week. We also hold daily 8:30 a.m. stand-ups in the East Wing conference room.

Welcome to the team. Your work directly supports safe, effective patient care.
