# Helios Ridge Solar — Residential Installation Readme

## Project Overview
This repository documents the 8.4 kW grid-tied photovoltaic installation completed at 442 Maple Hill Drive, completed on March 14, 2024. The system was designed for the Brennan family using 21 REC Alpha 400 W panels, a SolarEdge SE7600H inverter, and Tesla Powerwall 3 backup storage. Target annual production is 11,200 kWh, offsetting approximately 92% of the home's historical usage.

## System Specifications
- Module array: 21 × REC Alpha Pure-R 400 W (8.4 kW DC)
- Inverter: SolarEdge SE7600H-US with 99.2% CEC efficiency
- Storage: Tesla Powerwall 3, 13.5 kWh usable capacity
- Mounting: IronRidge XR100 rail, flash-mounted on composite shingle
- Monitoring: SolarEdge mySolarEdge and Tesla app integration

## Installation Timeline
Permitting with the County of Riverside began January 8, 2024, and approval arrived February 2. Crew mobilization started February 19; racking and DC wiring finished in four days. The utility interconnection agreement was signed March 5, and final PTO was granted March 14, 2024. Total on-site labor: 87 crew-hours.

## Performance & Warranty
Post-commissioning tests show 7.92 kW AC peak output and 98.6% system efficiency. REC offers a 25-year product and performance warranty; SolarEdge covers the inverter for 12 years, extendable to 25. The Powerwall carries a 10-year warranty with 70% retention guarantee.

## Maintenance Notes
Clean panels quarterly with deionized water. Inspect MC4 connections and rapid-shutdown devices every 12 months. Update SolarEdge firmware via the installer portal. Contact lead technician Marcus Chen at mchen@heliosridge.solar for service requests.
