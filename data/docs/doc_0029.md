# NexusGuard Summit 2025: "Zero-Trust Unveiled" Event Plan

## Overview and Objectives
NexusGuard will host its annual partner and media summit on March 14, 2025, at the Moscone Center in San Francisco. The primary goal is to debut the NexusGuard X9 endpoint protection suite and secure 200 qualified enterprise leads. We are allocating $185,000 for venue, AV production, and catering, with a targeted ROI of 4:1 within 90 days of the event closing.

## Venue, Logistics, and Timeline
The program runs from 09:00 to 17:00 PST, followed by a networking reception until 19:30. Registration opens at 08:00 in Hall B, where attendees will receive RFID badges pre-linked to the NexusGuard demo environment. Key milestones include:
- January 10, 2025: Vendor contracts finalized
- February 28, 2025: Speaker deck reviews completed
- March 7, 2025: Final headcount confirmed (target: 340 guests)

## Agenda and Content
Dr. Elena Vasquez, Chief Security Architect, will deliver the keynote at 10:30, followed by live red-team simulations led by Marcus Chen. Breakout tracks cover cloud posture management, ransomware recovery, and compliance automation.

## Marketing and Outreach
Our campaign launches on January 20, 2025, across LinkedIn, targeted email, and partner co-marketing channels. We will deploy three distinct audience tracks:
- CISOs and VP-level security leaders
- Managed security service provider partners
- Technology journalists and industry analysts

## Budget and Success Metrics
Total event spend is capped at $185,000. Success will be measured by 200 qualified leads, 12 media placements, and 85% attendee satisfaction. Post-event, the sales team will begin follow-up within 48 hours to maintain momentum from the March 14 launch.
