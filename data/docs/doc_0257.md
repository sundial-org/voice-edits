# Lab Notes — Q3 Protein Expression Run

## Project Overview
This document records progress on Project Lyra, a recombinant enzyme platform led by Dr. Elena Vasquez. The objective is to produce a thermostable variant of cellulase CBH-II for pilot-scale biomass conversion at the Meridian facility.

## Batch Preparation (2024-09-12)
We prepared 12 L of modified TB medium with 50 µg/mL kanamycin and 0.2 mM IPTG induction. All glassware was autoclaved at 121 °C for 20 minutes. Inoculation used a 200 mL overnight starter culture of E. coli BL21(DE3) carrying plasmid pLyra-9.

## Fermentation & Sampling
Growth was monitored every 4 hours. Key observations:
- OD600 reached 0.8 at hour 8, triggering induction.
- Temperature held at 37 °C for the first 6 hours, then reduced to 25 °C post-induction to improve solubility.
- Samples drawn at T=12, T=18, and T=24 hours for SDS-PAGE and activity assays.
- Total harvest volume was 10.4 L after accounting for evaporation and sampling loss.

## Purification & Analysis
Cell pellets were lysed using a high-pressure homogenizer at 15,000 psi. The clarified lysate was loaded onto a 5 mL HisTrap column. Elution fractions were pooled based on A280 peaks. Specific activity measured 42 U/mg against Avicel substrate at 60 °C.
