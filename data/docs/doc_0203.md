# Meridian Launches Real-Time Treasury Insights for Mid-Market Firms

On October 14, 2024, Meridian rolled out a new real-time treasury dashboard designed specifically for finance teams managing between $10M and $250M in annual revenue. After eighteen months of development with partners at Ridgeway Capital and the Meridian Labs engineering group in Austin, the feature addresses a persistent blind spot: fragmented cash visibility across multiple banking partners.

## The Visibility Gap

Most mid-market companies still rely on end-of-day spreadsheets exported from three or four separate banking portals. By the time a controller spots a liquidity squeeze, the window for action has already narrowed. Meridian's 2023 user survey found that 62% of respondents waited more than 48 hours to consolidate multi-account balances. That delay costs real money—especially when interest rates shift or vendor payments cluster at month-end.

## What Changes with Live Treasury

The new module pulls data every 90 seconds from connected accounts and translates raw balances into actionable forecasts. Rather than presenting static numbers, Meridian applies scenario modeling based on historical cash-flow patterns. Finance leaders can now stress-test against supply delays, seasonal dips, or sudden capital calls without leaving the platform.

Key capabilities include:

- Unified cash-position tracking across up to 12 banking relationships
- Automated 13-week rolling forecasts updated hourly
- Custom alert thresholds tied to covenant ratios or payroll deadlines
- Direct export to NetSuite and Sage Intacct for audit trails

## Early Results

A pilot program with 34 companies—including Meridian Labs client Portico Manufacturing—ran from June through September 2024. Participants reduced their cash-reporting cycle from an average of 3.5 days to under four hours. Portico's treasury team credited the dashboard with helping them avoid a $180,000 overdraft exposure during a vendor consolidation in July.

## Security and Compliance

Every data connection runs through Meridian's SOC 2 Type II infrastructure, audited last quarter by Baker Tilly. Multi-factor authentication and role-based access are mandatory; no read-only exceptions exist. For firms in regulated sectors, audit logs retain 7 years of transaction-level detail.

## Next Steps

Current Meridian subscribers can activate Real-Time Treasury from their workspace settings at no additional charge through December 31, 2024. New customers joining on the Standard plan will receive the module starting January 15, 2025. The product team plans to add cross-currency exposure tracking in Q2 2025, building on feedback gathered since the October release.

The era of reactive treasury management is ending. With live visibility, finance teams can finally lead with confidence rather than catch-up.
