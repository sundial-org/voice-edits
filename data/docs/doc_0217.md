# Helios Ridge Residential Solar — Phase II Installation

## Project Overview
This README covers the 14.2 kW DC grid-tied photovoltaic array commissioned for the Whitman family residence at 482 Emerald Hill Road. Designed by SunPeak Engineering and installed by Brightline Solar Contractors, the system went live on March 15, 2024, after passing all municipal and utility inspections.

## System Specifications
The roof-mount array uses 34 REC Alpha Pure 420 W modules paired with two SolarEdge SE7600H inverters. Key metrics include:
- Annual production target: 18,400 kWh
- Module efficiency: 21.8%
- Warranty coverage: 25 years on panels, 12 years on inverters

## Installation Timeline
Work began February 2, 2024, with racking and conduit completed by February 18. Electrical tie-in occurred March 4, followed by utility interconnection approval on March 12. Final commissioning and monitoring setup finished March 15.

## Safety and Compliance
Brightline Solar maintained zero lost-time incidents across 34 worker-days. All fall-protection gear met ANSI Z359.11 standards, and the array complies with 2023 NEC Article 690 guidelines.

## Monitoring and Maintenance
Homeowners access real-time production via the SolarEdge app. Scheduled maintenance includes:
- Biannual panel cleaning (April and October)
- Annual inverter thermal scan
- Five-year electrical torque-check of all DC connections
