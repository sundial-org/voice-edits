# Internal FAQ — Hollow Oak Interactive

Welcome to the team reference doc. If you just joined after the March 2024 restructure, or you have been here since the original prototype, this should cover the day-to-day confusion that Slack threads never fully resolve.

## Remote Hours & Core Availability

We are fully distributed across three time zones. Core overlap runs 10:00–14:00 EST, Tuesday through Thursday. Outside that window, async is the default. Mira Chen, our studio lead, checks messages once in the morning and once after dinner; do not expect an immediate reply outside overlap. If you are tracking hours for client invoicing, log them in Clockify under the project code GHO-2024.

## Project Pipeline: Ghost Orchard

Ghost Orchard is our primary title, currently targeting build 0.9.4 for internal playtesting on September 14. The milestone list is locked until then.

- Narrative pass led by Juno Reyes completes by August 22
- Environmental art for the cemetery biome must be merged before August 29
- Sound design for the bell puzzle is outsourced to Deep Signal Audio

Do not push feature branches to main after 18:00 on merge days. The CI pipeline runs nightly at 02:00 EST, and broken builds wake up the Berlin contractor.

## Tools, Assets, and Version Control

We use Unity 2022 LTS and Perforce for source control. Asset Store purchases over $50 require approval from finance; anything smaller comes out of the monthly $300 discretionary pool. Keep textures in the shared drive under `/GhostOrchard/Assets/Source/` with the naming convention `GHO_Biome_Object_Version`. If you downloaded a free kit on itch.io, still log it in the Asset Registry so licensing does not surprise us during the October 2024 audit.

## Crunch Policy and Wellness

We do not do sustained crunch. The studio policy allows a maximum of four consecutive days with extended hours, and only with 48-hour notice and written consent. After any extended sprint, the following Monday is a mandatory recovery day—no meetings, no Slack pings, no exceptions. If you feel pressure to violate this, talk to Mira or submit an anonymous note through the HR portal. We have lost good people to burnout before; we are not repeating that pattern in 2024.
