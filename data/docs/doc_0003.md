# Internal FAQ — Helios Residential Solar Installers

## Project Lead Times & Scheduling
As of June 2024, our standard rooftop installation window is 6 to 8 weeks from contract signature. Commercial arrays over 500 kW, such as the March 2024 Meridian Logistics project, require an additional 3-week engineering review. Project manager Dana Reeves assigns crews every Monday; if a homeowner requests a Friday start, we need 72-hour notice to reorder the pallet delivery.

## Material Shortages & Substitutions
We are currently substituting Enphase IQ8+ microinverters with SolarEdge Power Optimizers on jobs under 25 modules due to supply constraints expected through August 2024. The change does not affect the 25-year panel warranty, but it does alter the monitoring portal login. Keep the SKU swap form in the truck binder and have the homeowner initial line 4.

## Safety & Site Protocols
Every crew member must wear Class E hard hats and harnesses anchored to structural rafters rated for 5,000 lbs. In January 2024, technician Marcus Chen identified a compromised truss on the Oak Street residence; the repair added $1,200 but prevented a fall hazard. Report all structural concerns to the site lead before unloading panels.

## Customer Communication Rules
- Confirm the arrival window via text by 7:00 PM the night before.
- Never discuss pricing adjustments at the door; redirect to Dana Reeves.
- Provide the homeowner with the 48-hour post-install inspection checklist.
- If weather delays exceed 2 hours, call the dispatcher immediately.
- Leave the yard cleaner than found; magnet sweep for all nails and staples.

## Warranty & Post-Install Follow-Up
Module warranties run 25 years; workmanship coverage is 10 years. The inverter warranty varies by brand: Enphase covers 25 years, SolarEdge covers 12 years with an option to extend to 25 for $350. File all serial numbers in the project folder within 24 hours of commissioning, or accounting will flag the job as incomplete.
