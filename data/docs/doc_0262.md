# Meridian Pay — Product Requirements Document v2.4

## Overview
Meridian Pay is a B2B embedded payments platform enabling mid-market SaaS vendors to offer branded invoicing and same-day settlement. This PRD covers the Q3 2025 release targeting the North American corridor, led by product owner Sarah Lin, with engineering kickoff on July 14, 2025.

## Goals & Success Metrics
We aim to reduce merchant onboarding friction and increase net revenue retention. Key targets include achieving a 92% automated KYC pass rate, keeping median settlement latency under 4 hours, and growing active merchant accounts to 8,500 by September 30, 2025. Success will also be measured by a Net Promoter Score above 42 within the first 90 days post-launch.

## Functional Requirements
The platform must support multi-currency wallets, real-time sanctions screening, and role-based access controls. Core user flows include:

- Instant vendor onboarding with identity verification completed in under 90 seconds
- Automated invoice generation with PCI-DSS Level 1 tokenization
- Same-day ACH and wire settlement options for transactions up to $250,000
- Configurable payout schedules with daily, weekly, or monthly cadence
- Audit-grade reporting exports compatible with QuickBooks and Xero

## Technical & Compliance Constraints
All data must reside in US-East and US-West regions with AES-256 encryption at rest. We are required to maintain SOC 2 Type II attestation and adhere to FinCEN guidelines updated March 2025. Integration partners include Plaid for bank linking and Sila for money transmission licensing. The architecture must support 10,000 concurrent settlement requests with 99.99% uptime.

## Timeline & Milestones
Design review is scheduled for June 27, 2025. Beta access opens to 120 pilot merchants on August 4, 2025, followed by general availability on September 15, 2025. A post-launch retrospective is set for October 3, 2025, to evaluate settlement speed, fraud rates, and merchant adoption velocity.

## Open Questions & Risks
Regulatory approval for embedded lending features remains pending with state regulators in California and New York. We also need to confirm pricing tiers—currently proposed at 0.35% per transaction—before the August pilot. Engineering has flagged potential latency issues with the Plaid sandbox that must be resolved by July 28, 2025.
