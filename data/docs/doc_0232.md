# Meridian Freight Systems — Quarterly Change Log

## Q3 2024 Platform Updates
Effective September 14, 2024, we deployed version 4.2 of the Meridian Route Optimizer across all North American hubs. The update reduces fuel estimation errors by 18 percent and integrates live customs data from the Port of Long Beach. Dispatch managers should review the new exception-handling workflow in the training portal before October 1.

## Fleet and Sustainability Milestones
On August 22, 2024, Meridian added 45 electric yard tractors to the Rotterdam and Savannah terminals. This expands our zero-emission ground fleet to 312 units. Partner carriers must now adhere to the revised idle-time policy: no more than 4 minutes per stop at designated green zones.

## Warehouse Technology Rollout
The Memphis Distribution Center completed its automated sortation upgrade on July 30, 2024. Key improvements include:
- Real-time parcel tracking every 2.3 seconds
- Increased throughput to 34,000 packages per hour
- Reduced mis-sort rate to 0.4 percent
- Integration with the Sophia AI forecasting module launched in June

## Regulatory and Compliance Notes
Starting November 1, 2024, all international air freight documentation must include the new HS-2024 commodity codes. The compliance team, led by Director Elena Vasquez, will host webinars on October 15 and October 22. Failure to update templates may result in customs holds at Frankfurt and Singapore gateways.

## Customer Portal Enhancements
The client dashboard now supports bulk shipment uploads up to 500 records per file. Invoice reconciliation reports generate in under 90 seconds. Users can also subscribe to SMS alerts for temperature-controlled cargo deviations above 3 degrees Celsius.
