# MetroLink Transit Authority — Internal FAQ (Q3 2024)

## Upcoming Service Changes Effective October 14
Beginning Monday, October 14, all operators assigned to the North Corridor must report to Bay 7 rather than Bay 3 for pre-trip inspections. Route 42 will shift to 12-minute headways during peak hours, and the temporary shuttle linking Riverside Station to the Central Depot will be discontinued. Please update your route cards by Friday, October 11, and confirm your new bay assignment with Dispatch Supervisor Elena Vasquez.

## Safety Protocol Reminders
Every vehicle must complete the enhanced brake-check sequence added in March 2023. If a bus logs more than 4,500 miles without a recorded Level-B inspection, it is automatically flagged in the fleet dashboard. Operators are required to wear high-visibility vests during all platform assistance, including during daylight hours. Remember: no personal audio devices are permitted while operating in revenue service, per Policy 7.2 revised on June 1, 2024.

## Shift Bidding and Payroll Deadlines
The fall bid period opens September 30 and closes October 7 at 23:59. Seniority lists will be posted in the crew room by 06:00 on October 9. Overtime requests submitted after 14:00 on Thursday will roll into the following pay cycle ending Friday, October 18. If you notice a discrepancy in your hours, submit Form HR-104 within 5 business days.

## Equipment and Uniform Updates
New radio units have been distributed to Routes 17, 22, and 89. The remaining 312 units will arrive by November 15. Winter uniform orders must be placed through the portal by October 25; sizes not submitted by then will default to the measurements recorded in July 2023.

- Inspect your assigned radio for the updated firmware label (v4.1 or higher).
- Test emergency call buttons before leaving the depot each morning.
- Return defective headsets to Maintenance Window B with a completed tag.

## Who to Contact
For immediate operational issues, call the Control Center at extension 4100. Benefits questions go to Marisol Dent in Human Resources. All other inquiries can be directed to the internal helpdesk, monitored weekdays from 05:00 to 23:00.
