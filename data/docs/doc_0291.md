# Internal FAQ — Open Horizon Learning

## Program Launch Timeline
Our 2024–2025 rollout begins on September 9, 2024, when partner districts in Oakland and Denver go live. Dr. Amara Okonkwo finalized the pilot curriculum in July, and all facilitators must complete onboarding by August 23. If a school misses the window, they join the January 13 cohort instead.

## Tech Stack & Access
We run on Lighthouse LMS v4.2, hosted by CloudEd. Every student and mentor receives a unique SSO linked to their district email. If credentials fail:
- Clear cache and retry with `.edu` domain
- Submit a ticket to `help@openhorizon.org` with student ID
- Call the support line at (555) 019-8823 between 7 a.m. and 6 p.m. PT

## Funding & Budget Guardrails
The Mellon Foundation awarded us $1.2 million in March 2024. Of that, 60% is restricted to direct classroom technology. Unrestricted funds cover travel and the annual staff retreat in Portland (scheduled for November 14–16). Any expenditure over $5,000 requires dual sign-off from Marcus Chen and Priya Desai.

## Mentorship Protocols
Mentors check in twice weekly and log notes in the dashboard. Red flags—missing three consecutive logs, reported bullying, or sudden grade drops—trigger an automatic alert to Regional Lead Jamal Rivera. Do not promise families private tutoring; refer them to the approved partner list.

## Data Privacy & Compliance
We are FERPA-compliant and GDPR-aligned for our EU pilot in Lisbon. Student records are encrypted at rest using AES-256. Only staff with Level-2 clearance can export reports. In case of a breach, notify Elena Vasquez within one hour and initiate the incident response checklist.

## Common Questions From Staff
- Can I share my login? No—accounts are tied to background checks.
- When do stipends arrive? The 15th of each month via direct deposit.
- Is remote work allowed? Two days per week with manager approval.
- How do I request new hardware? Use Form 27-C in the staff portal.
- Who approves conference travel? Submit to the Program Ops team 30 days in advance.
