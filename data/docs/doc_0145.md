# Meridian Launches Real-Time Treasury for Mid-Market Firms

The gap between enterprise cash management and modern software has never been wider. At Meridian, we are closing it.

On March 14, 2024, we released Meridian Treasury, a unified dashboard designed for finance teams navigating volatile rates and fragmented banking relationships. Built after 18 months of collaboration with operators like Clara Mendez, CFO of Northwind Logistics, the platform replaces static spreadsheets with live liquidity visibility. For companies managing $4.2B in collective annual volume across our pilot cohort, the shift is immediate.

## What Changed

Traditional treasury tools require manual reconciliation across six or more banking portals. Meridian connects to over 240 institutional accounts through a single API layer, then applies machine-learning categorization to every transaction. The result is a cash position updated every 90 seconds rather than every 30 days.

Key capabilities include:

- Real-time consolidated balances across USD, EUR, and GBP accounts
- Automated sweep rules that trigger when idle cash exceeds $50,000
- Scenario modeling that stress-tests liquidity against rate hikes of 150 basis points
- Audit-ready reporting exports compatible with NetSuite and Workday
- Role-based permissions for controllers, treasurers, and board observers

## Why This Matters Now

Interest rate volatility in Q1 2024 erased the luxury of passive cash management. Mid-market companies—those with $20 million to $500 million in revenue—often lack dedicated treasury staff but face the same exposure as multinationals. Meridian Treasury targets that gap without requiring a six-figure implementation contract.

Mendez noted that her team reduced manual reconciliation by approximately 12 hours per week within the first month. That time was redirected into strategic forecasting rather than data entry.
