# Weekly Status Update — BrightPath Learning Foundation

## Program Highlights
This week our cohort of 42 tutors completed 1,180 virtual mentoring sessions across partner districts in Detroit and Atlanta. On March 14, we launched the updated algebra module for grades 7–9, developed in collaboration with Dr. Amara Okonkwo. Early feedback shows a 23% improvement in problem-set completion rates compared to the previous version.

## Partnerships & Outreach
We finalized a memorandum of understanding with the Riverside School District on Tuesday, expanding access to 3,200 additional students by fall 2025. Our team also presented at the National EdTech Equity Conference in Chicago, connecting with representatives from the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative and two regional community colleges.

## Operations & Metrics
- 847 active learners logged into the platform this week
- Average session duration increased to 34 minutes, up from 28 minutes in February
- Volunteer retention rate held steady at 91% through Q1
- Server uptime remained at 99.97% despite the Tuesday migration
- Three new curriculum designers joined the content team on March 13
