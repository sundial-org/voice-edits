# Meridian Climate Solutions: Q2 Product Launch & Stakeholder Summit

Meridian Climate Solutions will host its flagship Q2 Product Launch & Stakeholder Summit on March 14, 2025, at the Portland Convention Center. The event introduces our GridSync 4.0 platform to 400 invited guests, including utility partners, municipal planners, and institutional investors. Our objective is to secure commitments for three pilot deployments before the fiscal quarter closes on June 30.

## Event Objectives and Key Metrics

We aim to generate qualified partnership leads, demonstrate live carbon-accounting dashboards, and announce the $12 million Series B extension led by NorthBridge Ventures. Success will be measured against a target of 85 percent attendee engagement with the interactive demo pods and at least 15 signed letters of intent within ten business days.

- Confirm 400 registered attendees by February 28, 2025
- Secure venue catering and AV contracts with Cascade Events by January 10
- Deliver twelve live technical demonstrations across four themed stations
- Publish real-time emissions-offset tracking via the Meridian public dashboard
- Distribute follow-up surveys within 48 hours of closing remarks

## Program Agenda

Registration opens at 08:00, followed by a 09:30 keynote from Chief Executive Dr. Elena Vasquez. The midday session features a panel moderated by Portland Mayor Keith Wilson, exploring urban decarbonization mandates. At 14:00, attendees rotate through hands-on GridSync 4.0 workshops led by our engineering leads, Marcus Chen and Aisha Okonkwo. The program concludes with a 17:00 reception showcasing local zero-carbon catering vendors.

## Logistics, Budget, and Risk Management

The approved event budget is $340,000, allocated across venue rental ($95,000), production and AV ($110,000), marketing and invitations ($65,000), and contingency ($70,000). We will deploy a hybrid streaming option for remote stakeholders, expecting an additional 220 virtual participants. Weather-related travel disruptions are mitigated through partner hotel blocks at the Riverfront Grand and flexible rescheduling clauses in all vendor contracts.

## Communication and Follow-Up Protocol

Pre-event outreach begins six weeks prior, using segmented email campaigns and direct outreach from our partnership team. Post-event, the sales and policy teams will host virtual office hours every Tuesday and Thursday through April. All presentation recordings, carbon-impact reports, and partnership agreements will be uploaded to the Meridian portal by March 21, 2025, ensuring full transparency and sustained momentum into the second quarter.
