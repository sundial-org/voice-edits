# Ember & Oak Roastery

## About
Founded in 2017 by Marisol Vega in Portland, Ember & Oak is a small-batch roastery focused on direct-trade relationships and precise development. We operate out of a renovated 1924 warehouse in the Central Eastside, where we roast on a refurbished 1968 Probat UG-22.

## Sourcing
We work with six partner cooperatives across Guatemala, Ethiopia, and Colombia. Every lot is contracted before harvest and shipped in GrainPro liners to our facility at 442 SE Salmon Street. In 2023 we imported 14,200 kilograms of green coffee.

## Roast Profiles
- Costa Rica Las Lajas Black Honey: medium roast, 11 minutes, citrus and brown sugar
- Ethiopia Yirgacheffe G1: light roast, 9 minutes 45 seconds, jasmine and bergamot
- Colombia Huila Decaf: medium-dark, Swiss Water process, cocoa and almond
- Blend No. 4: rotating seasonal, roasted in 30 kg batches, shipped within 48 hours

## Wholesale & Brewing
We supply 34 cafés and restaurants in the Pacific Northwest. Orders placed before 2:00 PM Tuesday ship Thursday via FedEx Ground. We also offer barista training on the first Saturday of each month.
