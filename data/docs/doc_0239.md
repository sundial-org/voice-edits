# SentinelOne CyberDefense Summit 2025 — Event Plan

## Overview and Objectives
SentinelOne is hosting the CyberDefense Summit 2025 on March 14, 2025 at the Moscone Center in San Francisco. The single-day event targets CISOs, SOC managers, and channel partners across North America. Our primary objective is to generate 400 qualified pipeline opportunities while showcasing the Singularity XDR platform version 8.2. The agenda balances executive keynotes, hands-on labs, and peer networking to position SentinelOne as the autonomous endpoint leader.

## Venue, Logistics, and Timeline
Registration opens at 07:30, with the main stage program running from 09:00 to 17:30. We have reserved the West Hall for 850 attendees, including a dedicated partner pavilion for 12 sponsor booths. Catering will provide breakfast, a seated lunch, and an evening reception until 20:00. Audio-visual production is contracted through EventTech Pro, with redundant fiber uplinks and on-site NOC support.

## Marketing and Outreach Strategy
Pre-event promotion launches January 6, 2025 with a multi-channel campaign across LinkedIn, targeted email sequences, and partner co-marketing. We will deploy three registration waves: VIP CISOs on January 13, general admission on January 27, and partner guests on February 3. Content pillars include the "Ransomware Resilience Report 2024" and customer video testimonials from Vanguard Health and Meridian Logistics.

## Agenda Highlights
The morning keynote at 09:15 features CEO Tomer Weingarten unveiling the 2025 threat landscape forecast. Breakout tracks run in parallel:
- Autonomous Response in Critical Infrastructure
- Cloud Workload Protection Deep Dive
- Threat Hunting with AI Assist
- Channel Partner Enablement Workshop

Afternoon labs begin at 14:00 in Room 204, accommodating 60 participants per session with dedicated SentinelOne engineers.

## Risk Management and Contingencies
We maintain a $75,000 contingency reserve for weather, speaker cancellations, or AV failures. Backup speakers are confirmed for every session, and virtual streaming options will activate if in-person attendance drops below 500 registrants by March 7, 2025. Security screening will use badge verification and bag checks managed by VenueSafe staff.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 48 hours, all attendees receive personalized thank-you messages and session recordings. The sales development team will prioritize follow-up for 320 MQLs captured at check-in, with partner referrals routed through the PRM portal by March 17, 2025. A post-mortem report is due to the board by March 28, 2025.
