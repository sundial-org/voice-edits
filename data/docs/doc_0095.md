# Launch Event Plan: Meridian Pay Mobile Wallet Release

## Event Overview
Meridian Pay will unveil its v3.0 mobile wallet on March 14, 2025, at the Glass Tower in San Francisco. The 90-minute hybrid event targets 400 in-person guests and an estimated 12,000 virtual attendees. Chief Product Officer Elena Vasquez will deliver the keynote, followed by a live demo of the new biometric checkout flow.

## Venue and Logistics
The Glass Tower main auditorium holds 450 seated guests with dedicated fiber for live streaming. Registration opens at 4:30 PM PT; doors close promptly at 5:00 PM PT. Catering will be handled by Bay Bites, with dietary accommodations requested via the event portal by March 7, 2025.

- AV checks and sound balancing at 2:00 PM
- Speaker rehearsal with Elena Vasquez at 3:15 PM
- Security and badge printing at 4:00 PM
- Networking reception in the atrium until 7:30 PM

## Marketing and Invitations
Invitations will roll out in three waves: press and analysts on February 10, 2025; enterprise partners on February 17; and general users on February 24. Social assets will highlight the 0.8-second transaction benchmark demonstrated in internal testing. Target registration rate is 85 percent of capacity within ten days.

## Budget and Staffing
The total event budget is $145,000, allocated across venue rental ($42,000), production ($58,000), catering ($22,000), and contingency ($23,000). We require 18 on-site staff members, including four security personnel and two bilingual hosts to assist international guests.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 48 hours, attendees will receive a thank-you email containing the keynote recording and a survey. The virtual stream archive will remain accessible until April 14, 2025. Metrics for success include 2,500 new wallet activations within 30 days and a net promoter score above 42.
