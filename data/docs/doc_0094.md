# TerraSync Global: Launch Event Plan — Q3 2025

## Event Overview
On September 18, 2025, TerraSync Global will unveil its next-generation carbon capture platform at the Meridian Convention Center in Copenhagen. The all-day summit gathers 340 investors, policymakers, and engineering leads to witness the live deployment of the Atlas-9 unit. Chief Executive Officer Dr. Elena Vasquez will open the proceedings, followed by a keynote from Director of Operations Marcus Chen outlining the 2030 net-negative roadmap.

## Logistics & Venue Setup
The venue requires three staged zones: the main auditorium for 400 guests, a demonstration hangar for the Atlas-9 prototype, and a networking lounge serving locally sourced catering. Registration begins at 08:30 CEST, with doors opening at 09:30. AV contracts are finalized with Nordic Event Tech, and security clearances for the prototype area close by August 22, 2025.

## Program Highlights & Speakers
The agenda balances technical depth with strategic vision. Key sessions include:
- 10:00 — Opening remarks by Dr. Elena Vasquez
- 11:15 — Live Atlas-9 capture demonstration (15-minute cycle)
- 13:30 — Panel: Scaling Direct Air Capture in Northern Europe
- 15:45 — Funding round announcement: $120 million Series D close

Each session includes 10 minutes for moderated Q&A to maintain schedule integrity.

## Marketing & Outreach
Campaigns launch on July 14, 2025 across LinkedIn, industry newsletters, and the Copenhagen Climate Coalition network. Target demographics are venture capital partners with climate mandates, municipal energy directors from Denmark and Germany, and senior researchers from the Technical University of Denmark. Registration targets 85 percent capacity by September 1.

## Risk Management & Sustainability
The event adheres to a zero-single-use-plastics policy and offsets all attendee travel through TerraSync’s verified removal credits. Backup power is secured via the venue’s grid-connected battery array. In case of prototype delay, a pre-recorded 4K visualization of the capture cycle will substitute the live demonstration without altering the schedule.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 48 hours of closing, attendees receive a digital packet including technical white papers, speaker contact lists, and a survey measuring satisfaction and investment interest. The sales and partnerships team will schedule 30 follow-up meetings during the week of September 22, 2025, aiming to convert at least 20 percent of qualified leads into pilot agreements by Q4.
