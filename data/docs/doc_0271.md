# Autumn Skywatch Event Plan — Orion Astronomy Club

## Event Overview
The Orion Astronomy Club will host its annual Autumn Skywatch on Saturday, October 14, 2024, at Riverside Observatory Park. The gathering runs from 7:00 PM to 11:30 PM and is open to members and the public. Our goal is to observe the Orionid meteor shower peak, guide newcomers through basic constellation identification, and collect preliminary data for the club’s variable star monitoring project. We expect roughly 45 attendees based on last year’s turnout.

## Logistics and Setup
Dr. Elena Vasquez will serve as event coordinator, with Marcus Chen handling equipment checkout. We need to confirm parking access by October 2. The club will provide three 8-inch Dobsonian telescopes, two binocular mounts, and one dedicated camera rig for meteor tracking. Setup begins at 5:30 PM near the north lawn.

- Dobsonian telescopes (3 units)
- Binocular mounts and red-light lanterns
- Meteor camera rig with laptop
- Sign-in table and waiver forms
- Handouts featuring the October star chart

## Observation Schedule
The program is split into guided sessions and open viewing. From 7:30 PM to 8:45 PM, Dr. Vasquez will lead a tour of Pegasus, Andromeda, and the rising Pleiades. At 9:15 PM, we shift focus to the eastern horizon for Orionid meteors, with peak activity expected near 10:40 PM. A brief break at 9:00 PM allows for warm drinks and a 15-minute presentation on meteor shower mechanics.

## Weather Contingency
If cloud cover exceeds 70 percent by 6:00 PM on October 14, we will postpone to the backup date of October 21. Members will be notified via email and the club’s group message by 5:00 PM. In case of light rain without heavy cloud, we will move telescopes under the pavilion and continue binocular viewing.

## Outreach and Follow-Up
We will distribute 60 printed sky maps and collect contact information for future workshops. Photos taken by Marcus Chen will be uploaded to the club gallery by October 18. Newcomers interested in membership can sign up at a discounted rate of $25 for the remainder of the year. Our next meeting is scheduled for November 3 at the community library.
