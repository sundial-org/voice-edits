# HelioWorks Solar Regional Launch & Training Event Plan

## Event Overview
On March 15, 2026, HelioWorks Solar will host its annual installer certification and product unveiling at the Riverside Convention Center. The one-day event targets certified electricians, roofing contractors, and project managers across the Southwest region. We expect 180 attendees and aim to certify 45 new installers on our new 450 kW commercial racking system.

## Venue and Logistics
The Riverside Convention Center offers 12,000 square feet of exhibition space in Hall B, plus an outdoor demonstration yard for live roof-mount simulations. Registration opens at 7:30 AM, with the keynote beginning promptly at 9:00 AM. Parking is complimentary for vendors in Lot C; attendees should use the South Garage at $12 per day.

## Agenda and Key Sessions
- 9:00 AM — Welcome and market outlook from CEO Dana Whitmore
- 10:30 AM — Hands-on racking workshop with engineer Marcus Chen
- 12:30 PM — Networking lunch featuring regional project case studies
- 2:00 PM — Safety certification exam and panel Q&A

## Marketing and Outreach
Promotion begins February 1, 2026 through email campaigns, trade journal ads, and partner co-marketing with GreenTech Supply. We will distribute 500 printed brochures and offer early-bird pricing of $199 through February 28. Social updates will highlight the 450 kW system demos twice weekly.

## Staffing and Budget
Event coordinator Priya Nair will oversee eight staff members, including three technical trainers and two registration volunteers. The total event budget is $38,000, with $14,000 allocated to venue rental and catering, and $9,000 reserved for demonstration equipment transport and insurance.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 72 hours of closing, the sales team will send personalized certification results and schedule one-on-one consultations for attendees interested in the March pilot program. All feedback surveys must be submitted by March 22, 2026 to qualify for the continuing-education credit.
