# SolarEdge Pro Installer Portal — Product Requirements Document

## Overview
Helios Residential is building the SolarEdge Pro Installer Portal to replace fragmented spreadsheets and email chains for our 140 certified partner installers. The platform must support end-to-end project tracking from site survey through PTO, reducing average installation cycle time from 42 days to under 28 days by Q3 2025.

## User Personas
- Lead Installer (Maria Chen): Manages 12 crews across the Southwest and needs mobile-first job cards.
- Project Coordinator (David Okonkwo): Handles permit submissions for municipalities including Austin Energy and PG&E.
- Warehouse Manager (Rosa Delgado): Tracks inverter and racking inventory across three distribution hubs.

## Functional Requirements
The portal must support the following capabilities:
- Real-time job scheduling with GPS-verified crew dispatch
- Automated permit document generation for 50+ jurisdictions
- Digital signature capture for homeowner interconnection agreements
- Inventory reservation tied to BOM validation within 24 hours
- Integration with Enphase and SolarEdge monitoring APIs
- Role-based dashboards for sales, ops, and finance teams

## Technical Specifications
The stack will run on AWS us-west-2 with a React frontend and PostgreSQL backend. All data must be encrypted at rest using AES-256. The mobile app targets iOS 16+ and Android 13+ with offline sync for areas with poor coverage. System uptime SLA is 99.9%, with nightly backups retained for 90 days.

## Milestones and Timeline
- Alpha release: March 15, 2025 (internal beta with 8 installers)
- Beta rollout: May 1, 2025 (expand to 40 partners in California and Texas)
- General availability: July 1, 2025 (all 140 partners onboarded)
- Post-launch audit: August 30, 2025 to validate the 28-day cycle target

## Success Metrics
We will measure adoption via daily active users, with a target of 85% of active crews logging in within 30 days of rollout. Support tickets related to scheduling errors must drop below 5 per week. Revenue impact will be tracked through faster PTO approvals, aiming for $2.4M in accelerated billing by year-end 2025.
