# Internal IT FAQ — Meridian General Hospital

## Access & Account Lockouts
If your clinical account is locked after three failed login attempts, do not call the service desk immediately. Instead, wait 10 minutes for the automatic reset cycle to complete. For urgent access during active patient care, page the on-call engineer at extension 4421 and reference ticket category "CLIN-URGENT". All password resets require verification against your employee ID and a supervisor confirmation for privileged roles such as Radiology PACS admin or Pharmacy EHR super-user. As of the March 14, 2024 policy update, shared service accounts must be rotated every 90 days.

## Clinical System Downtime
The electronic health record (EHR) is maintained under vendor contract with Cerner Millennium build 2023.2. Scheduled maintenance windows occur every Sunday from 02:00 to 04:30 EST. If you observe latency above 800ms in the OR scheduling module, escalate to the Infrastructure team before notifying nursing supervisors. Dr. Elena Vasquez, Chief Medical Informatics Officer, must approve any emergency downtime protocol that exceeds 30 minutes.

## Incident Escalation Path
Use the following priority matrix for all after-hours events:
- P1: Life-safety or active surgery disruption — call immediately
- P2: Department-wide degradation — page within 15 minutes
- P3: Single-user or non-critical — log ticket by 08:00 next business day
During declared Code Black events, all non-essential updates are frozen until the Emergency Operations Center issues an all-clear.

## Equipment and On-Call Coverage
The IT equipment cage in Basement Level B requires badge access assigned by Security. Laptops for clinical float pools are tracked via asset tag beginning with MGH-IT-. If you borrow a replacement workstation, return it by 18:00 Friday to avoid inventory flagging. The night shift on-call rotation is managed by Marcus Chen; his contact sheet is posted on the SharePoint portal under Operations > Schedules.

## Security and Compliance Reminders
Never store patient identifiers on local drives or USB devices. All remote access requires multi-factor authentication through the Meridian VPN gateway. Report suspected phishing attempts to security@meridianhealth.org within one hour. Quarterly audits are conducted by the Compliance Office; the next review begins on June 1, 2024.
