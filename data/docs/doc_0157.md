# Internal FAQ — Resonance Collective

## Who can access the shared studio?
Membership is open to producers and engineers who have completed the onboarding session hosted every first Thursday by Marco Velez. As of March 2024, we maintain 14 active keycards. Guests must be registered 48 hours in advance via the internal portal.

## Booking windows and cancellation policy
Studio A and Studio B can be reserved up to 30 days ahead. If you cancel within 6 hours of your slot, you forfeit that session and it counts toward your monthly 12-hour limit. The 8 p.m. to 2 a.m. block is reserved for collective projects only, not solo work.

## Gear maintenance and reporting
All shared equipment is audited on the 15th of each month. If a patch cable fails or a preamp clips unexpectedly, log it in the #gear-alert channel immediately. Replace consumables—like the Neumann U87 windscreen or DI boxes—from the supply closet, not from personal stock.

- Check phantom power before connecting condensers
- Wipe down faders with isopropyl after tracking vocals
- Return microphones to labeled foam slots by 11 p.m.

## Mixing and mastering approvals
No track may be released under the Resonance Collective imprint without passing the peer review stage. Submit rough mixes to the shared Dropbox folder named RSC_2025_Q2 by the Friday before our listening session. Three members—currently Juno Park, Darius Cole, and Lena Ortiz—must sign off on dynamics, stereo imaging, and metadata.

## Royalties and collaboration splits
Revenue from collective releases is split 60/40 between the primary artist and the collective fund. Collaborative tracks require a signed split sheet before mastering begins. Payments are distributed on the 1st and 15th through our accountant, Maya Torres. Keep all session files archived for a minimum of 2 years.

## Conflict resolution and next steps
Disputes over studio time or credit should first be addressed in the weekly roundtable held every Tuesday at 7 p.m. If mediation fails, the steering committee—formed in January 2023—will issue a binding decision within 5 business days. Questions? Reach out to the internal board or email faq@resonancecollective.internal.
