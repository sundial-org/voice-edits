## Access & Credentials
Access to the SentinelOne Management Console requires MFA through our Okta identity provider. If your token expires or you are locked out, do not create a secondary account. Instead, submit a ticket via the internal ServiceNow portal and reference the escalation code SOC-8841. Root-level API keys rotate every 90 days; the next rotation is scheduled for January 9, 2025. Engineers must verify their key fingerprints in the vault before running automated playbooks.

## Incident Escalation Protocol
When a confirmed ransomware behavior is detected, the responder must isolate the endpoint within 5 minutes and page the on-call engineer. The escalation matrix distinguishes between low-confidence malware (log and observe), confirmed exfiltration (notify legal within 2 hours), and active encryption (trigger full IR). All actions are timestamped in the case management system for audit review by the compliance team.

## Common Pitfalls
Analysts often forget to disable automatic remediation in sandbox environments, which can corrupt test artifacts. Always verify the environment tag—PROD, STAGING, or LAB—before applying a kill chain response. Remember: isolation is reversible; deletion of forensic snapshots is not. When in doubt, preserve evidence and ask in #soc-escalation.
