# SentinelOne Endpoint Defense: Q3 2024 Field Notes

## Threat Landscape Overview
Since the March 2024 disclosure of CVE-2024-21893, adversaries have accelerated exploitation of edge appliances. In our telemetry across 1,400 enterprise endpoints, ransomware dwell time dropped to a median of 4.2 days—down from 11 days in late 2023. The SentinelOne Singularity Platform detected 68% of these intrusions during the initial access phase, prior to payload delivery.

## Detection Engineering Updates
On August 14, 2024, the Research Team released Storyline v9.2. The update introduces behavioral correlation for living-off-the-land binaries, specifically targeting `rundll32.exe` abuse in Windows 11 23H2 environments. Key improvements include:
- Real-time mapping of parent-child process trees across 12,000 agent nodes
- Automated IoC extraction with 94.7% precision on sandbox detonations
- Native integration with MITRE ATT&CK v14.1 technique IDs T1059.001 and T1071

## Incident Response Playbook: Finance Sector
A regional bank in Frankfurt contacted our SOC on September 2 after anomalous LSASS memory reads triggered a Critical alert. Analysts isolated the host within 90 seconds using one-click containment. Forensic review revealed a custom Cobalt Strike beacon configured with a 300-second sleep jitter. Remediation required rotating 47 service accounts and patching three unpatched Exchange servers running build 15.02.1544.

## Metrics and Roadmap
In Q3 we processed 2.3 petabytes of endpoint telemetry. Our false-positive rate for behavioral rules remains below 0.8%. Looking ahead to the November 2024 Singularity release, we will expand cloud-native workload protection to Kubernetes clusters running version 1.29 and above. Field engineers should complete the updated certification module by October 15.
