## Core Defensive Principles
Every endpoint agent must maintain three non-negotiable behaviors regardless of organizational size:
- Continuous kernel-level telemetry with sub-50 millisecond ingestion
- Autonomous rollback capabilities without cloud dependency
- Behavioral AI models updated every 72 hours via the Singularity Data Lake

These controls align with NIST 800-53 Rev. 5 and reduce mean time to contain (MTTC) from 287 minutes to under 12 minutes in benchmark deployments.

## Case Study: Meridian Health
On 3 September 2024, Meridian Health detected an anomalous PowerShell child process spawning from `svchost.exe` at 02:14 UTC. SentinelOne’s Storyline technology mapped 14 cross-process events in 4.2 seconds, isolating the host before encryption touched 1,847 patient records. The adversary used a stolen certificate issued to "CyberForge Tools LLC" in March 2023.
