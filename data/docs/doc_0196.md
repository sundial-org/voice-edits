# Product Requirements Document — Meridian Roast Co. Signature Blend Launch

## Overview
Meridian Roast Co. will release its flagship single-origin offering, the "Abyssinian Sunrise," on March 14, 2026. The roastery aims to move 4,200 units in Q2 across direct-to-consumer and wholesale channels led by Head Roaster Elena Vasquez.

## Target Audience
The primary buyer is a 28–40-year-old specialty coffee drinker seeking traceable, high-elevation beans with bright acidity and caramel finish. Secondary buyers include cafés in Portland and Denver requiring 5 lb bulk bags.

## Technical Specifications
- Roast profile: medium-light, 412°F drop temperature
- Bag sizes: 12 oz retail, 5 lb wholesale
- Shelf life: 45 days from roast date stamped on valve bag
- Packaging: compostable kraft with nitrogen flush

## Launch Milestones
- January 20, 2026: Final cupping approval
- February 10, 2026: Packaging print run complete
- March 1, 2026: Warehouse stock received
- March 14, 2026: Public launch and press release

## Quality & Compliance
Every batch must pass SCA cupping with a score above 84.5. The sourcing team verifies direct-trade contracts with the Yirgacheffe cooperative. Traceability codes link each bag to harvest lot 2025/ET-09.

## Marketing Requirements
Campaign assets must highlight Elena Vasquez's 14 years of roasting experience and the 1,850-meter elevation of the source farm. Social copy needs to reference the March 14 debut and include tasting notes of bergamot, black tea, and burnt sugar.

## Success Metrics
We target 4,200 units sold by June 30, 2026, with a 35% gross margin and wholesale penetration in at least 18 independent cafés. Customer retention should reach 22% within 90 days of first purchase.
