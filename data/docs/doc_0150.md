# Welcome to Meridian Commerce: Merchant Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Meridian Commerce platform. This guide walks you through activating your store, connecting inventory, and going live within 48 hours of account verification.

## Account Setup and Verification

After receiving your welcome email on September 15, 2024, log in to the Merchant Dashboard using your assigned Organization ID. Complete identity verification by uploading your business registration and linking a verified bank account. Our compliance team reviews submissions within one business day. Once approved, you will gain access to the Catalog Builder and Order Management tools.

## Catalog Integration

Import products using our REST API, CSV uploader, or direct Shopify connector. Each SKU requires a unique merchant reference number, high-resolution images, and accurate weight dimensions for shipping calculations. We recommend starting with your top 50 products to test workflows before scaling to full inventory.

Before publishing, review these requirements:

- Product titles must be under 120 characters and free of promotional language
- Images need a minimum resolution of 1200 by 1200 pixels
- Pricing must include currency formatting for USD, EUR, or GBP
- Inventory counts should reflect real-time stock levels via webhook updates
- Shipping profiles must specify handling time within 2 business days

## Launch Checklist

Once your catalog is imported, configure taxes, enable payment processing through Stripe or Meridian Pay, and activate fraud protection filters. Run a test transaction using the sandbox environment. When you are satisfied, toggle your store status to "Live" in the Settings panel. Your storefront will be visible to customers within 15 minutes.

## Post-Launch Support

Our Merchant Success team, led by director Elena Vasquez, monitors new launches for the first 30 days. You can reach support through live chat from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST, or email onboarding@meridiancommerce.com. We maintain a 99.9% uptime SLA and provide weekly analytics reports every Monday morning.

Congratulations on joining Meridian Commerce. Your onboarding window closes on October 15, 2024, after which standard support tiers apply.
