# Welcome to the Meridian Astronomy Club: New Member Onboarding

## Getting Started
Founded in 1987 by Dr. Elena Vasquez, the Meridian Astronomy Club has grown into a community of over 140 stargazers. To activate your membership, please complete the online orientation by October 15 and attend one orientation session at the Oak Ridge Observatory.

## Equipment & Safety
We maintain a shared inventory of telescopes, including the 12-inch Schmidt-Cassegrain and three portable Dobsonians. Before borrowing gear, complete the safety checklist and sign the liability waiver.

- Review the telescope setup guide
- Attend a hands-on training session
- Return equipment by 9:00 PM on loan nights

## Meeting Schedule & Events
Club meetings occur every first and third Thursday at 7:30 PM in the Hawthorne Community Center. Our annual dark-sky retreat takes place November 4–6 at Black Mesa Park.

## Communication & Resources
Join our Slack workspace and subscribe to the monthly newsletter edited by Marcus Chen. For questions, contact the board at board@meridianastro.org.

We look forward to exploring the cosmos with you. Clear skies!
