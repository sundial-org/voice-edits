# Meridian Freight Solutions — Internal Operations README

Meridian Freight Solutions coordinates multimodal cargo transport across North America and Europe. This repository documents our internal dispatch platform, tracking APIs, and driver compliance modules maintained by the engineering team led by Director of Logistics Technology Sarah Chen.

## System Overview

The platform processes approximately 14,000 shipments daily across 38 regional hubs. Built on a modular microservices architecture, it integrates real-time GPS telemetry, customs documentation, and automated billing workflows. The core stack was modernized in March 2024 to reduce latency in route-optimization queries by 41 percent.

## Key Modules

- Dispatch Router Engine: calculates fuel-efficient paths for the 2,400-vehicle fleet
- Customs Documentation Generator: produces EU and US customs packets in 14 languages
- Driver Compliance Tracker: monitors hours-of-service regulations updated in January 2023
- Client Portal API: exposes shipment status to partners including Apex Manufacturing and Nordex Logistics

## Deployment & Access

Production environments run in the Frankfurt and Chicago data centers. Engineering staff should request VPN credentials through the IT portal managed by Marcus Delgado. All deployments require peer review and sign-off from the Operations Security team before reaching staging.

## Metrics & Roadmap

Since the Q2 2024 rollout, median delivery times have dropped to 26.4 hours for cross-border routes. We aim to integrate predictive weather rerouting by November 2025 and expand coverage into the Iberian corridor with 12 additional partner carriers.
