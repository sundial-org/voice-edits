# Helix Bio Labs Quarterly Innovation Showcase — Event Plan

## Overview & Objectives
On March 14, 2025, Helix Bio Labs will host its Quarterly Innovation Showcase in the atrium and Conference Centers B and C at the Cambridge Research Park campus. The purpose is to highlight recent breakthroughs in CRISPR-based therapeutics, present preliminary data from the Phase II Meridian Trial, and foster collaboration with external partners. Dr. Elena Vasquez, Chief Scientific Officer, will deliver the opening remarks at 09:00, followed by poster sessions and a moderated panel on regulatory pathways. We expect approximately 240 attendees, including internal scientists, venture partners, and representatives from the FDA liaison office.

## Agenda & Program Flow
The event runs from 08:30 to 16:00. Registration opens at 08:30 in the main lobby. At 09:30, Dr. Vasquez introduces three project leads who will present 15-minute talks on vector optimization, in vivo delivery, and biomarker discovery. The midday break begins at 12:15, allowing guests to network and review 18 interactive posters. The afternoon panel at 13:45 features Dr. Samuel Okonkwo discussing manufacturing scale-up targets for Q3 2025. Closing remarks are scheduled for 15:45, with a guided tour of the new GMP suite available until 16:00.

## Safety, Logistics, and Compliance
Because this is an active R&D facility, all attendees must complete biosafety orientation upon arrival.

- Government-issued photo ID and pre-registration confirmation required at check-in
- Lab coats, safety glasses, and closed-toe shoes mandatory for the GMP suite tour
- Visitor badges must remain visible; escorted access only beyond the yellow demarcation lines
- Photography restricted in Bay 4 and the cleanroom corridor per IP protocols

Facilities will lock exterior doors at 08:00 for setup and reopen for departure at 16:30. AV technicians will test microphones and projection systems by 08:45.

## Catering and Hospitality
Catering will be provided by GreenStem Hospitality, with coffee service available from 08:30. A buffet lunch at 12:15 includes vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergen-labeled options. Water stations will be placed near each poster cluster. Alcohol will not be served, consistent with laboratory safety policy.

## Post-Event Follow-Up
Within 48 hours of March 14, the communications team will distribute a digital summary, contact lists, and slide decks to all registered guests. The facilities team will conduct a deep clean of Conference Centers B and C by 18:00. Feedback surveys close on March 21, 2025.
