# Meridian Freight & Logistics Changelog

## Q2 2024 Release — Network Expansion & Digital Upgrades

On March 14, 2024, Meridian Freight completed the integration of the Denver Central Cross-Dock, adding 340,000 square feet of climate-controlled storage and reducing average Midwest transit times by 18 hours. Operations Director Elena Vasquez led the rollout across three regional hubs, with zero lost-time incidents during the 11-day transition window.

Key infrastructure updates include:
- Activation of real-time GPS telemetry across 1,247 active trailers
- Deployment of the Aurora TMS v4.2 platform for automated bill-of-lading generation
- Expansion of the Atlanta-Savannah express lane to accommodate 22% higher container volume
- Integration of carbon-offset tracking directly into client dashboards

The Denver facility began receiving inbound rail freight on March 22, 2024, with full operational certification achieved by April 2.

## Carrier Partnerships & Rate Adjustments

Effective May 1, 2024, Meridian renegotiated primary carrier contracts with J.B. Hunt, Schneider National, and XPO Logistics. The revised agreements lock in flat fuel surcharges through Q3 and introduce guaranteed capacity clauses for peak season shipments exceeding 500 pallets per week.

Notable adjustments:
- Dry van spot rates reduced by 4.3% on lanes over 800 miles
- Refrigerated contract minimums increased from 12 to 18 loads per month
- Expedited air charter options now available through the Meridian AirBridge program, with 6-hour pickup windows in 14 metro markets

Clients enrolled in the Enterprise Logistics Program will see updated pricing reflected in invoices dated on or after June 1, 2024.

## Sustainability & Compliance Milestones

Meridian achieved ISO 14001:2015 recertification on April 18, 2024, following a third-party audit of the Portland, Memphis, and Houston distribution centers. The audit documented a 31% reduction in warehouse energy consumption since January 2023, driven by LED retrofit programs and optimized HVAC scheduling.

Additional milestones:
- Launch of the Meridian Green Fleet pilot, deploying 48 electric terminal tractors at the Chicago hub
- Partnership with the Environmental Protection Agency’s SmartWay Transport program, effective June 2024
- Publication of the 2023 Carbon Disclosure Report, available to stakeholders by request

Compliance updates also include revised hazardous materials handling protocols aligned with the Department of Transportation’s 2024 emergency response amendments.

## Client Portal & Service Updates

The Meridian Client Gateway received a major update on May 15, 2024. Users now have access to predictive ETAs powered by machine-learning forecasts trained on 2.3 million historical delivery records.

Service-level commitments have been revised as follows:
- Standard ground: delivery within 3-5 business days
- Priority ground: delivery within 2 business days
- White-glove installation: 48-hour scheduling with dedicated crew assignment

For technical support or contract inquiries, contact the Logistics Support Center at 1-800-555-0199 or via the updated portal messaging system.
