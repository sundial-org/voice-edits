# Mothlight Games — Velvet Hollow

Mothlight Games is a three-person indie studio based in Portland, Oregon, founded in late 2022. Our debut title, *Velvet Hollow*, is a narrative-driven exploration game set in a decaying forest observatory. We believe small teams can build worlds that feel vast, strange, and deeply human.

## About the Project

*Velvet Hollow* began development in March 2023 after a successful prototype funded through our community. Over the past 14 months, we have rebuilt the lighting engine twice, recorded 340 minutes of original ambient audio with composer Elias Vance, and hand-painted every biome using a custom watercolor shader pipeline. The game follows Dr. Ione Reed, an astronomer returning to her childhood research station to archive the last signals from a silent satellite network. There is no combat. Progress comes from listening, mapping, and deciding what to preserve.

## Core Features

- Non-linear exploration across six interconnected biomes, each tied to a different season of the station's history
- A dynamic soundscape that reacts to player movement, with 22 unique instruments recorded live
- Handwritten journal system with over 180 discoverable entries from previous researchers
- Accessibility options including full controller remapping, colorblind modes, and narrated UI
- Estimated playtime of 6–9 hours for a single path, with multiple endings shaped by archival choices

## Development Timeline

We are currently in the final polish phase. The beta build is scheduled for August 15, 2024, with a targeted release on Steam and itch.io for November 2024. Our QA process involves a closed group of 40 external testers who have already logged 1,200 hours across earlier builds. We publish monthly development logs on our site and welcome direct feedback through our Discord server.

## The Team

Mothlight operates without a traditional office. Lead designer and programmer Sarah Jun handles systems and narrative integration. Artist and technical director Marcus Okoro builds our tools and visual pipeline. Audio lead and producer Elias Vance manages sound, music, and community outreach. Together we share every task, from bug fixes to marketing copy, because we believe authorship should remain visible.
