# Mosslight Games — Development Changelog

## Studio Overview
Founded in 2019, Mosslight Games is a four-person indie studio based in Halifax. We are currently shipping our second title, *Velvet Hollow*, a narrative exploration game built in a custom engine. This changelog covers the cycle from August through October 2024.

## Engine & Tools: v2.4.1 Patch
On October 14, 2024 we pushed build v2.4.1 to our internal branch. The update addresses three long-standing pipeline issues that affected asset streaming on lower-end hardware.

- Reduced texture load times by 34% through asynchronous mipmapping
- Fixed a race condition in the dialogue system that caused 12 reported soft-locks
- Added debug telemetry for Celeste Hollow’s shader compiler
- Rebuilt the save architecture to support cloud synchronization

These changes stabilize the foundation before we enter feature freeze on November 3.

## Content Milestones
The writing team finalized Act II of *Velvet Hollow*. Lead designer Mara Okonkwo delivered 23,000 words of branching dialogue and four new environmental puzzles set in the Saltmere Archives. Composer Jonas Pruitt recorded 47 minutes of original score using a modular synthesis rig in our Halifax studio.

We also completed motion-capture for the protagonist’s idle and traversal animations, reducing foot-slide artifacts by 80% in internal playtests.

## Community & Playtesting
Our closed beta expanded from 120 to 310 participants on September 22. Feedback has been overwhelmingly focused on pacing in the second act and the accessibility of the map interface.

Notable contributions from the community include:
- A screen-reader compatibility proposal adopted into UI build 211
- Localization files for Brazilian Portuguese and Korean submitted by volunteer teams
- Performance profiling from user "threadbare_fox" that isolated a GPU memory leak

We are reviewing all submissions for the v2.5.0 milestone.

## Upcoming Roadmap
Feature freeze is scheduled for November 3, followed by a four-week QA sprint. We aim to submit *Velvet Hollow* for certification by December 15. The public demo, currently running on build v2.4.0, will be updated to v2.4.1 before the end of the month.

If you are a backer or press contact, reach out to hello@mosslight.games for access logs and press kits. Thank you for following the project.
