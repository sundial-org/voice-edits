# BridgeEd Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the team. BridgeEd is a nonprofit edtech organization founded in 2014 to close digital literacy gaps in underserved school districts. Whether you are joining as an engineer, curriculum designer, or community coordinator, this guide will help you navigate your first month with clarity and purpose.

## Your First Week

Your onboarding starts on Monday, October 14, 2024, with a half-day orientation at our downtown hub. You will meet your direct manager, receive your laptop and access credentials, and tour the learning labs we operate in partnership with Roosevelt Middle School. By Wednesday, please complete the security and child-safety training modules in our learning portal. These are non-negotiable for anyone who will interact with students or handle district data.

## Tools and Access

We rely on a focused stack to keep operations lean and transparent. Once IT provisions your accounts, you will use:

- **Slack** for daily team communication and the #help-bridge channel
- **Notion** for project documentation, meeting notes, and the curriculum library
- **GitHub** for engineering contributions and open-source tool maintenance
- **Zoom** for remote tutoring sessions and partner check-ins

Please enable two-factor authentication on every account and store passwords in the shared vault, not in personal notes.

## Our Programs and Impact

BridgeEd currently serves 3 districts and reaches approximately 1,200 students each semester. Our flagship initiative, the Digital Bridge Program, pairs volunteer mentors with middle-schoolers to build foundational coding and critical-thinking skills. All staff members are encouraged to observe at least one tutoring session within their first 30 days. Seeing students engage with the material firsthand is the best reminder of why our work matters.

## Getting Involved Beyond Your Role

Nonprofit work thrives on collaboration. Within your first quarter, consider joining one of our internal working groups: Curriculum Equity, Volunteer Training, or Data Privacy. You do not need prior expertise—just curiosity and a commitment to our students. Our Executive Director, Maria Chen, hosts an open office hour every Thursday at 2:00 p.m. for questions about strategy, funding, or career growth.

## Questions and Next Steps

If anything in this guide is unclear, reach out to your onboarding buddy or email people@bridgeed.org. We are glad you are here. Your ideas and energy will directly shape how we deliver equitable technology education in the years ahead.
