# Welcome to BrightPath Learning: New Team Onboarding Guide

## Our Mission and Your Role
BrightPath Learning is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2014 to close literacy gaps for students in grades K-5. We partner with 42 public schools across the Midwest to deliver adaptive reading software and trained literacy coaches. As a new program associate, you will support deployment, train teachers, and monitor student progress data. Your work directly impacts over 8,000 learners each academic year.

## First Week Priorities
Your manager, Dr. Elena Vasquez, will schedule your orientation for Monday, March 4, 2024. Before then, please complete the following:

- Review the BrightPath Code of Conduct and sign the confidentiality agreement in your welcome packet
- Set up your accounts for Salesforce, LitTracker, and the staff Slack workspace (#general and #programs)
- Attend the virtual safety training hosted by our Operations Lead, Marcus Chen, on Wednesday at 10:00 a.m. ET

## Tools and Daily Workflow
You will spend most of your time in LitTracker, our proprietary dashboard built in 2019. Each morning, check the "Priority Schools" queue to see which partners have flagged technical issues or low engagement scores. Document all site visits in Salesforce within 24 hours of completion. Please use the shared Google Drive folder "2024 Program Resources" for lesson templates and coach handouts.

## Support and Growth
We believe onboarding is ongoing. Every new hire is paired with a peer mentor—this quarter, that is Senior Coach Amara Okonkwo. You will meet biweekly for your first 90 days. In addition, all staff attend the annual BrightPath Summit on June 12, 2024, where we share research updates and set goals for the upcoming school year. If you have questions about benefits or payroll, contact hr@brightpathlearning.org.
