# Helios Solar Annual Installer Field Summit — Event Plan

## Overview and Objectives
On March 14, 2026, Helios Solar will host its annual Installer Field Summit at the Apex Convention Center in Denver, Colorado. The goal is to align our 45 certified technicians with updated NEC 2023 code changes, new racking hardware from SunLock Systems, and refined fall-protection protocols. Project Lead Marcus Chen will open the session with a review of Q1 installation metrics, emphasizing our target to complete 320 residential arrays by June 30.

## Venue and Logistics
The Apex Convention Center offers 12,000 square feet of climate-controlled exhibition space and secure outdoor staging for live equipment demos. Registration begins at 07:30, with the main program running from 08:30 to 16:00. We have reserved Rooms B and C for classroom modules, plus the East Lawn for hands-on rooftop simulations. Parking is validated for all attendees; carpooling is encouraged, with shuttle service departing the Helios headquarters at 06:45.

## Agenda and Training Modules
The schedule balances technical instruction with practical application. Each module is led by a senior engineer or certified safety officer.

- 08:30 – Welcome and Q1 metrics with Marcus Chen
- 09:15 – NEC 2023 updates and permitting workflows
- 10:30 – SunLock Systems rapid-mount demonstration
- 12:00 – Catered lunch and vendor networking
- 13:15 – Fall-protection refresh and harness inspection drills
- 14:45 – Live array build on simulated 6/12 pitch roof
- 15:45 – Certification renewal and closing remarks

## Safety Protocols and Equipment
Every attendee must present a valid OSHA 30 card at check-in. Personal protective equipment—including Class E hard hats, insulated gloves, and tethered tool lanyards—will be inspected before the live build. The East Lawn demo uses weighted ballast frames rather than structural penetrations to protect the venue surface. Emergency medical staff will be on-site, with the nearest trauma center, Denver General, located 3.2 miles south on Blake Street.

## Marketing, Registration, and Follow-Up
Invitations went to 58 field staff on February 2, 2026; we require confirmed RSVPs by February 27 to finalize catering and printed manuals. Post-event, Marcus Chen’s team will distribute digital certificates within 48 hours and update the internal training portal with video recordings of the SunLock demonstration. Feedback surveys will close March 21, and results will inform the September refresher course.
