# Helios Ridge Residential Solar Installation — Project README

This repository documents the complete design, permitting, and build records for the 14.2 kW rooftop photovoltaic system installed at 4820 Canyon Vista Drive, Austin, Texas. The project was executed by Helios Ridge Solar Co. between March 12, 2024 and April 28, 2024, with final utility interconnection completed on May 3, 2024 by Austin Energy.

## System Overview

The array consists of 34 REC Alpha 420W panels wired in two strings of 17, feeding a SolarEdge SE11400H-US inverter with power optimizers at each module. The south-facing roof pitch is 22 degrees with minimal shading from the neighboring live oak. Annual production is modeled at 19,850 kWh using PVWatts, offsetting approximately 92% of the homeowner's historical 21,400 kWh annual consumption.

## Installation Timeline

- March 12: Site survey and structural assessment by lead engineer Maria Chen
- March 29: Austin Energy interconnection application submitted
- April 9-18: Rack and rail mounting, DC wiring, and inverter installation
- April 22: City of Austin electrical inspection passed
- April 28: System commissioning and homeowner orientation

## Materials and Components

- 34 × REC Alpha 420W monocrystalline panels (12.4% module efficiency)
- 1 × SolarEdge SE11400H-US hybrid inverter with integrated EV charger readiness
- 34 × SolarEdge P505 power optimizers
- IronRidge XR100 rail system with FlashFoot 2 attachments
- 200A AC disconnect and production meter per Austin Energy spec

## Performance Monitoring

Post-commissioning data through the SolarEdge monitoring portal shows consistent production. The system generated 1,640 kWh in its first full month (June 2024). Homeowners can access real-time metrics via the mobile app and will receive quarterly maintenance checks through the five-year service agreement signed on installation day.
