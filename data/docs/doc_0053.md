# St. Mercy Health System — Clinical IT Changelog

## Q3 Infrastructure Upgrades — Completed 09/14/2024
The network backbone in the East Wing was upgraded over the weekend of September 14, led by senior engineer Marcus Chen. We replaced aging core switches at nodes 4B and 7C, reducing latency for PACS image retrieval by 38 percent. All radiology workstations were migrated to the new VLAN by 05:30 on Sunday without impacting the morning shift.
